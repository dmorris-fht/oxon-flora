### *Euphrasia pseudokerneri* Chalk Eyebright 
Pugsley
#### Ecology
A small annual hemiparasite of lime-rich short grassland with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles confined to the chalk and limestone of southern England. In Oxfordshire on the chalk, and only rarely on the limestone.
#### Trends
From a total of c. 26 localities, during 2000-22 six in Oxfordshire and five in vc23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
