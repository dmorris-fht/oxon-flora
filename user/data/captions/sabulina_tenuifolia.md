### *Sabulina tenuifolia* Fine-leaved Sandwort 
(L.) Rchb.
#### Ecology
An annual of dry places on light alkaline infertile soils (mainly chalk and limestone) in grass, tracks, arable margins and artificial habitats free of competitors.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in England but mostly on the chalk between Wiltshire and East Anglia.
#### Trends
Recorded by Druce (1927) at c. 17 sites, and by Killick et al. (1998) at 20 tetrads, but only confirmed during 2000-22 at Chinnor Chalk Pit.
#### Conservaton work
None
