### *Lythrum portula* Water-purslane 
(L.) D.A.Webb
#### Ecology
A prostrate annual of seaonally wet or damp places, on slightly acidic and low nutrient soils.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in the British Isles except northwaest Scotland. In Oxfordshire restricted to lime-rich areas - the Cotswolds, the Corallin Ridge and the Chilterns.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported ten sites and Killick et al. (1998) 11 tetrads in vc 23. In 2000-22 at 10 sites in the county and eight in vc 23.
