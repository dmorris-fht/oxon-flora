### *Rumex maritimus* Golden Dock 
L.
#### Ecology
This small annual, or short-lived perennial,  grows in damp alkaline grasslands with very high nutrient levels.
#### Status & distribution
As well as being coastal in the east, it is distributed widely in central England, and scattered elsewhere in the British Isles. In Oxfordhire mainly in the clay vales.
#### Trends
Nationally it has increased in frequency since 1962. In 1927 Druce reported eight sites, including Port Meadow, Binsey and Otmoor, and Killick et al. (1998) had four tetrads and Crawley (2005) none. Not found in 2000-22.
