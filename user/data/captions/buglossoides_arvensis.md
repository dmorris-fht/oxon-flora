### *Buglossoides arvensis* Field Gromwell 
(L.) I.M.Johnst.
#### Ecology
This annual grows in arable fields, open grass and waste ground, on light, dry, especially calcareous soils. Its seeds are short-lived.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles this is a plant of central, southern and eastern England. In Oxfordshire mostly in the Cotswolds and the Chilterns.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at 37 sites and to Killick et al. (1998) at 58 tetrads in vc 23. In 2000-22 at nine localities in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
