### *Linum radiola* Allseed 
L.
#### Ecology
This slender annual favours acidic and extremely low nutrient, open, damp grasslands.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly coastal on the northern, western and southern seaboards. confined to the chilterns plateau in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported it a Binfield Heath. Killick et al. (1998) cited two tetrads both on limestone and so unlikely.
#### Conservaton work
None. An approach has been made to the owners of Binfield heath, but without success; the site is densely shaded by Rhododendron
