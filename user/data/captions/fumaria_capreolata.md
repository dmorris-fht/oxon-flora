### *Fumaria capreolata* White Ramping-fumitory 
L.
#### Ecology
Annual neophyte, sometimes a winter annual. Mostly in urban watse places.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles, but rare in the northwest of Scotland and the southeast Midlands of England. In Oxfordshire mostly a casual in Oxford.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed seven places, all in Oxford. Killick et al. (1998) listed one during 1968-98, and three have been reported in 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
Monitored at its main site, City Farm, Eynsham.
