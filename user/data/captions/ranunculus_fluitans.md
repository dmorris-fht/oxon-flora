### *Ranunculus fluitans* River Water-crowfoot 
Lam.
#### Ecology
An aquatic usually of slightly alkaline and high nutrient, flowing water.
#### Status & distribution
Widely distributed in England and Wales, but rare in Scotland and almost absent from Ireland. Widely distributed in Oxfordshire, but mainly in the north and west.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 18 sites in vc 23, saying it was a "characteristic feature in the Thames from Kelmscott to Sonning"; Killick et al. (1998) said that the few specimens were "mostly scrappy, flowerless and damaged" and its decline in the main Thames is substantial. In 2000-22 recorded from c. 16 sites.
