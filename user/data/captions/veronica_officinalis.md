### *Veronica officinalis* Heath Speedwell 
L.
#### Ecology
This small, low-growing perennial prefers dry, acidic, short or open grassland, and flourishes with moderate nutrients, often growing on ant-hills.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles but declining in the Midlands. In Oxfordshire it is most frequent on the Chilterns plateau, but also grows on the Coralian Ridge and the Cotswolds.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) recorded 44 sites and Killick et al. (1998) 126 tetrads. During 2000-22 at c.78 sites in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
