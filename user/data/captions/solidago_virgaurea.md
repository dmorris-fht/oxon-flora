### *Solidago virgaurea* Goldenrod 
L.
#### Ecology
This is a genetically variable perennial herb which used to be valued for medicinal purposes. It grows in a range of habitats preferring free-draining usually acidic and nutrient poor soils.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles except the east Midlands; its distribution is obscured by confusion with American species planted as ornamentals.
#### Trends
Nationally it declined mostly post-1950, perhaps reflecting eutrophication; Braithwaite et al. (2006) found a further significant decrease in 1987/8 – 2003/4. Druce (1927) listed six sites in vc 23 and one in vc 22 Oxon.  Perhaps only at College Wood, Woodcote.
#### Conservaton work
None.
