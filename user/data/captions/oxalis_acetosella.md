### *Oxalis acetosella* Wood-sorrel 
L.
#### Ecology
A small, early-flowering, woodland perennial preferring acidic soil with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Common throughout the British Isles, expect the Fens. In Oxfordshire common on the Chilterns plateau and less frequent on the Cotswolds and Corallian Ridge, and rare elsewhere.
#### Trends
Druce wrote it was widely distributed and locally abundant in the county. Killick et al. (1998) cited 129 tetrads. Still ubiquitous on the Chilterns plateau, common in the Cotswold woods and on the Corallian Ridge.
#### Conservaton work
None.
