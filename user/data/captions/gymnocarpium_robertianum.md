### *Gymnocarpium robertianum* Limestone Fern 
(Hoffm.) Newman
#### Ecology
This fern requires damp limestone rocky places with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
In Britain as a wild plant it is uncommon, mostly in the west; in southeast England it has sporadically escaped from gardens, and these distributions meet in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Formerly only at Cornbury Quarry and Shotridge Wood; more recent records are probably of naturalized plants, but one unconfirmed record in 1981 at Shotridge Wood.

None.
