### *Juncus compressus* Round-fruited Rush 
Jacq.
#### Ecology
This rhizomatous perennial flourishes on wet neutral meadows and disturbed sites with slightly raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles, but commonest in the southern Midlands. Widespread in Oxfordshie, but mostly on the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 25 sites and Killick et al. (1998) cited 37 tetrads; during 2000-22 c. 30 localities have been recorded in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
The new ponds on Shrike Meadow (Farmoor by Eynsham, centroid SP 4384 0623) have a naturally colonised population of Juncus compressus - now usually around five plants every year - but never in the same place! First record 2016. Not seen 2019. Recorded 2020, 2021, 2022.
