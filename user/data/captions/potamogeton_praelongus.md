### *Potamogeton praelongus* Long-stalked Pondweed 
Wulfen
#### Ecology
This species requires deep water (>1 m), flowing or still, neutral pH and tolerates slightly raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in Scotland and Ireland, but rarely recorded in England. In Oxfordshire mostly along the Thames.
#### Trends
Druce 91927) described it as locally common, but Killick et al. (1998) reported only five tetrads, and most recently in the 1970s at Sandford and Port Meadow.
#### Conservaton work
None.
