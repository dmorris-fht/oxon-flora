### *Inula helenium* Elecampane 
L.
#### Ecology
This persistent perennial was grown widely in the British Isles for its ornamental, medicinal and veterinary properties. It favours neutral soils with slightly raised nutrients
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles. Widespread in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed seven sites, and Killick et al. (1998) 11 tetrads, and in 2000-22  ten sites in Oxfordshire and four  in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
