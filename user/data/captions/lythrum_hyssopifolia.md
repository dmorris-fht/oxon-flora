### *Lythrum hyssopifolia* Grass-poly 
L.
#### Ecology
A small annual of seasonally flooded open ground. It grows on neutral pH and slightly raised nutrient soils.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in south eastern England. Scattered in Oxfordshire; at one site since 1825.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported seven sites, and Killick et al. (1998) none extant in vc 23. Recently (2021) two new sites in the River Ray catchment.
#### Conservaton work
Monitored at Cholsey where the owner poughed, but didn't sow, an arable corner liable to flooding.  Seed collected for the MSB at Kew.
