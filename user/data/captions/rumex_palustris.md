### *Rumex palustris* Marsh Dock 
Sm.
#### Ecology
This annual to short-lived perennial grows in wet, nutrient-rich mud exposed in summer and autumn, but often flooded in winter.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in southeast England,scattered elsewhere, but absent from Ireland. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Reported by Druce (1927) from Otmoor in 1881 till 1884. None reported by Killick et al. (1998). found in 2014 by J A Webb on Port Meadow. In 2000-22 known in the county at three sites, 2 in vc23 and one in vc 22.
#### Conservaton work
This species appeared in scrapes made at Farmoor by the EA.
