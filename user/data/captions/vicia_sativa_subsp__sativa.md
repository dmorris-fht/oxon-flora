### *Vicia sativa subsp. sativa* Common Vetch 
L.
#### Ecology
This robust annual was a fodder crop, see discussion in Killick at al. (1998) and Killick (1975).
#### Trends
This subspecies declined sharply when farm horses were replaced by tractors (Killick 1975 http://archive.bsbi.org.uk/Wats10p285.pdf; Killick et al. 1998). Druce said it "survived as a relic of cultivation in all districts, but does not appear to establish itself."Killick et al. (1998) gave only one localitiy. but in 2000-22  c. 24 localities, so perhaps formerly under-recorded.
