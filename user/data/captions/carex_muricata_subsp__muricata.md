### *Carex muricata subsp. muricata* Large-fruited Prickly-sedge 
L.
#### Ecology
It grows on dry calcareous soil, usually in light shade.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles only in a few scattered sites in England, notably Gloscestershire and Wales. In Oxfordshire in 2000-22  it has been confirmed in five sites in the Chilterns, and was recorded pre 2000 in two sites in the northern  Cotswolds.
#### Trends
Recent work has revealed three (four) new sites in the norhern Chilterns.
