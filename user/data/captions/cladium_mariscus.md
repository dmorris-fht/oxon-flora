### *Cladium mariscus* Great Fen-sedge 
(L.) Pohl
#### Ecology
A large rhizomatous perennial of calcareous mesotrophic peat fens in England, but in acidic conditions in Scotland. In Oxfordshire only ever known at two sites.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles this species is very scattered but commoner in Ireland, the western seabord of Scotland and East Anglia.  In Oxfordshire frequent in peat cores but  in 20th century only ever at two sites, both in vc 22.
#### Trends
Last seen at its only two sites in vc 22 Oxon at Cothill SAC in 1982, and Peat Bottom Wood, Hatford 1965 by M Corley.
#### Conservaton work
Material from Swaddywell Pit, Northamtonshire, has been propagated.  There is a long established introduced population in the University Parks, Oxford.
