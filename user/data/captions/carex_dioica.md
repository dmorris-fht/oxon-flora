### *Carex dioica* Dioecious Sedge 
L.
#### Ecology
This diminutive sedge prefers neutral, often base rich, but very low nutrient mires; especially on sillty soil by flushes or springs.
#### Status & distribution
Abundant in Scotland and on the Burren, in Angelsey and the Broads. Rare on the Corallian Ridge in Oxfordshire
#### Trends
Much declined in England before 1930. Thought lost as none were reported in Lye Valley SSSI  since 1794 and none in vc 22 Oxon since Frilford SSSI in 1946.  In 2011 at Lye Valley SSSI and Frilford SSSI, refound by Judy Webb, doubtless benefitting from recent management.
#### Conservaton work
Refound recently at two sites, both following management work. There are male plants in the west and female at the Lye Valley  SSSI - some help is needed.
