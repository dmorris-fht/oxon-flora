### *Galeopsis speciosa* Large-flowered Hemp-nettle 
Mill.
#### Ecology
This arable annual is a neophyte which prefers neutral pH and tolerates high nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread, especially in Scotland, northern England, Wales and the fenlands, where it was a weed of root crops.
#### Trends
Always rare, Druce (1927) listed six sites and Killick et.al. (1998) three tetrads in vc 23. Braithwaite et al. (2006) found it decreased significantly between 1987-8 and 2003-4, especially on non-peaty soils. None during 2000-22
