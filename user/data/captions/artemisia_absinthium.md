### *Artemisia absinthium* Wormwood 
L.
#### Ecology
This aromatic perennial was grown for medicinal and flavouring purposes. Now it is a casual of open anthropogenic sites, and flourishes with moderate alkalinity and extremely high nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widely distributed in the British Isles, but mostly in central England. Widely distributed in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed ten sites and Killick at al. (1998) 33 tetrads. In 2000-22 five sites as an alien.
