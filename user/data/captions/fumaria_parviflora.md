### *Fumaria parviflora* Fine-leaved Fumitory 
Lam.
#### Ecology
This arable annual favours highly alkaline soils with slightly raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Almost entirely restricted to the chalk of southeast England. In Oxfordshire on the Chilterns scarp and the Wessex Downs.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) gave three localities, Killick et al. (1998) two tetrads, and in 2000-22 at two sites.
#### Conservaton work
None.
