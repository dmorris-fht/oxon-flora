### *Chenopodiastrum murale* Nettle-leaved Goosefoot 
(L.) S.Fuentes, Uotila & Borsch
#### Ecology
This is an annual which prefers nutrient-rich rubbish tips.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in southern England and Wales. Widespread in Oxfordshire, on the lime-rich soils.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) had nine sites, Killick et al. (1998 p.112) 20 tetrads. Two records in 2000-22. Rubbish tips and dunghills are now covered.
#### Conservaton work
None.
