### *Torilis arvensis* Spreading Hedge-parsley 
(Huds.) Link
#### Ecology
This annual, or rarely biennial, was once frequent on heavy but well-drained calcareous, autumn-sown cereal fields, and now in set-aside.
#### Status & distribution
Absent from Scotland and Ireland, it was mostly a plant of the southeast as far north as Scarborough. Widespread in Oxfordshire except for the Wessex Downs and the clay vales.
#### Trends
It has had a huge decline, being vulnerable to herbicides and a poor competitor. Druce (1927) listed 41 sites and Killick et al. (1998) 41 tetrads. During 2000-22 at four localities in Oxfordshire and two in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
Monitored sporadically with the adjacent  Grass poly Lythrum hyssopifolia site.
