### *Potentilla argentea* Hoary Cinquefoil 
L.
#### Ecology
This is a small perennial  of extremely low nutrient, dry, acidic grassland. It copes well in short and longer grassy conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Rare in the British Isles, except in the southeast where it is frequent. In Oxfordshire on the Corallian Ridge and the Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed four sites, and Killick et al. (1998) three; in 2000-22 at five in Oxfordshire and one in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
