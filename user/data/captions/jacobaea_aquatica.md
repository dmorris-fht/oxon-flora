### *Jacobaea aquatica* Marsh Ragwort 
(Hill.) P.Gaertn.
#### Ecology
A perennial of neutral wet grassland, tolerant of raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles, except at high altitude. Widespread in Oxfordshire except for the Wessex Downs.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) wrote "Common and generally distributed in the river valleys. In Oxford meadows" but did not give localities. Killick et al. (1998) cited 107 tetrads. During 2000-22 found at c. 43 sites in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
