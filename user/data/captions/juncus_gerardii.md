### *Juncus gerardii* Saltmarsh Rush 
Loisel.
#### Ecology
This coastal plant is very rare inland only occurring at saline places, as for instance in Cheshire.
#### Status & distribution
Found on all coasts of the British Isles, this rush  is very localized inland. In Oxfordshire only at the Marcham Salt Spring LWS where salty water rose from the Jurassic strata of the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Known at Marcham Salt Spring from 1881 till 1956, then no records till 2015 by Judy Webb.
#### Conservaton work
None.
