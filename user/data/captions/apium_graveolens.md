### *Apium graveolens* Wild Celery 
L.
#### Ecology
A small biennial of neutral, damp grasslands, often in saline and high-nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the Brisitsh isles, but commonest around the coast and at saline sites inland. Once widespread in Oxfordshire,
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed eight sites and Killick et al. (1998) 12 tetrads. During 2000-22 only at two sites, but only seen recently at Marcham.
#### Conservaton work
At Marcham Salt Spring LWS in vc 22 the plant has been known as part of a suite of salt tolerant plants since 1853. It is being actively managed by the landowner with a three year rotation of cultivation of three strips. The site is designated a County Wildlife Site.
