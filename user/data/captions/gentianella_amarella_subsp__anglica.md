### *Gentianella amarella subsp. anglica* Early Gentian 
(Pugsley) T.C.G.Rich & McVeigh
#### Ecology
This tiny spring-germinating annual grows on shallow, very low nutrient, calcareous soils with short open sward on parched south-facing slopes.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly on the southwestern chalk of England, but scattered in the east and central areas.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported var. praecox at Ewelme, and Killick et al. (1998) only at Taynton; in 2000-22 five in Oxfordshire and one in vc23.
#### Conservaton work
OFG and the National Trust have done some monitoring at Whitehorse Hill and found numbers very variable.
