### *Poa infirma* Early Meadow-grass 
Kunth
#### Ecology
This little annual is frequent in trampled coastal grassland in southwest England and is now spreading more widely in road verges, pavements and gutters., faviouring neutral soils with slightly raised nutients.
#### Status & distribution
Frequent in coastal grassland in southwest England, recently spreading into southern and eastern England.
#### Trends
Now known at three roadside sites.
