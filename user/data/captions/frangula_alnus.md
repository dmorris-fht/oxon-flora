### *Frangula alnus* Alder Buckthorn 
Mill.
#### Ecology
This small tree of wet ground flourishes in neutral to slightly acidic mesotophic soils.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles mostly in southern, central and eastern England, and in southern Wales and central Ireland.
#### Trends
Only one site, Waterperry Wood, was reported by Druce, several others may be native (especially in areas rarely visited by Druce) eg Sarsden (S E Erskine 2004), Wychwood (H J M Bowen 1986). Widely planted since.
#### Conservaton work
Often planted.
