### *Potamogeton perfoliatus* Perfoliate Pondweed 
L.
#### Ecology
It prefers calcareous rivers with raised nutrients, but is also found in ponds and reservoirs.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread throughout the British Isles. In Oxfordshie mostly in the River Windrush and the Thames.
#### Trends
Druce reported this species as "common, generally distributed in our rivers, ditches, ponds and canals". Killick et al. (1998) reported 31 tetrads. In 2000-22 only at three localities in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
