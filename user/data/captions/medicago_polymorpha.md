### *Medicago polymorpha* Toothed Medick 
L.
#### Ecology
This is a small annual of waste places which prefers dry neutral soils with raised nutrients. Casual inland.
#### Status & distribution
Only native in coastal areas. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) and Killick et al. (1998) both reported two sites, and twosites (bothin vc 22 Oxon) were known during 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
None.
