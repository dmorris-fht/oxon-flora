### *Scandix pecten-veneris* Shepherd's-needle 
L.
#### Ecology
This annual grows chiefly on arable over calcareous clays with modrate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Once widespread in south and east England. In Oxfordshire it was formerly widespread.
#### Trends
This plant has suffered a huge decline, which is quite recent in Oxfordshire. Druce gave it as "Common and generally distributed." Killick et al. (1998) noted  24 tetrads saying "it has declined everywhere." In  2000-22 13 localities in the county.
#### Conservaton work
The latest find in vc 22 at Hatford in 2012, was on a bund in gravel/sand workings. Efforts are being made to introduce the seed to another part of the pit.
