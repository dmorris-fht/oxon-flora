### *Montia fontana* Blinks 
L.
#### Ecology
A dwarf annual or perennial of seasonally or permanently wet places on acid or neutral sand or gravel.
#### Status & distribution
Common throughout the British Isles except for the central Midlands. In Oxfordshire mostly on the leached uplands.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 18 sites in vc23, and Killick et al. (1998) had 11 tetrads; and ten during 2000-22 in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
None.
