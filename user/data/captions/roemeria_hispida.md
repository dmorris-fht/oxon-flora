### *Roemeria hispida* Rough Poppy 
(Lam.) Stace
#### Ecology
Rough Poppy grows in dry arable land, mostly on calcareous soils with moderate nutrients, often with other poppies.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly on the chalk of southern England. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
While Druce listed 14 sites, Killick et al. (1998)  had 21 tetrads; and 2000-22 BSBI had 10.
#### Conservaton work
None.
