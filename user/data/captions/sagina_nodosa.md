### *Sagina nodosa* Knotted Pearlwort 
(L.) Fenzl
#### Ecology
This small perennial grows in damp places with base-rich low-nutrient water, and also in calcareous grasslands.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, mostly in the north and west. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
In southern England in general it has suffered a big decline since the 1950s, presumably due to agricultural intensification. Druce (1927) listed 14 sites, and Killick et al. (1998) five tetrads; not reported since 1984.
#### Conservaton work
None.
