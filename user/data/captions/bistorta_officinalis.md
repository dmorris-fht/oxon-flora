### *Bistorta officinalis* Common Bistort 
Delarbre
#### Ecology
This showy perennial grows in neutral waterside and marshy places, tolerating high nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles. Widely distributed in Oxfordshire, but probably widely introduced.
#### Trends
Druce noted it was "Very rare and perhaps the relic of ancient culture." but he reported 19 sites and Killick et al. 14 tetrads, and 2000-22 at 13 sites, but Crawley (2005) indicates that most records are of introduced populations. Sometimes persistent - it was recorded by Sibthorp (1795) near Gosford Bridge and again there by David Morris in 2020.
