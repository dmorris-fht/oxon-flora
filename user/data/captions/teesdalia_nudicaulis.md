### *Teesdalia nudicaulis* Shepherd's Cress 
(L.) W.T.Aiton
#### Ecology
This small winter annual is found in well drained sandy soils, preferring bare or disturbed ground and extremely low nutrients. Experiments in Germany have shown that it is unable to respond to raised nitrogen levels (Fichtner & Schultze 1992).
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles. In Oxfordshire on the Cotswolds, Corallian Ridge and Chilterns.
#### Trends
Nationally it has suffered a large decline, and in Oxfordshire Druce (1927) reported it at one site, Killick et al. (1998) reported no records in vc 23 but Poingdestre had recorded it at Hermitage in 1977; and 2000-2022 only two sites, both in vc22.
#### Conservaton work
Monitoring at Frilford reported it last in 2009.
