### *Menyanthes trifoliata* Bogbean 
L.
#### Ecology
An aquatic of shallow, slow-moving water and in acidic bogs with low nutrients. Often planted as an ornamental.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, but mostly in the northwest. Widespread in Oxfordshire, mostly introduced.
#### Trends
Reported by Sibthorp from behind Headington Hill and the Lye Valley (Bullingdon Bog) where it still grows. Often planted as an ornamental. Druce (1927) reported it at 28 sites and Killick et al. (1998) at 17.
#### Conservaton work
Scrub has been removed from Lye Valley SSSI; re-introduction is proposed following clearance on Boars Hill.
