### *Equisetum sylvaticum* Wood Horsetail 
L.
#### Ecology
This graceful perennial is widespread in Wales, Scotland and the west of England, but more restricted in the south. It requires damp, shady conditions and prefers slightly acidic soils, tolerating raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in Wales, Scotland and the west of England, but more restricted in the south. In Oxfordshire, only on the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Only ever seen once and not rerfound near Shotover.
#### Conservaton work
None.
#### Variation
In several places especially on Boars Hill, a very branched for of E. arvense is easily mistaken for E. sylvaticum.
