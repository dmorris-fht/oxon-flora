### *Carex elata* Tufted-sedge 
All.
#### Ecology
This sedge grows in marshy habitats and on river banks, often lime- and nutrient-rich, and seasonally flooded.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in England and Ireland with local strongholds, e.g. in East Anglia. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
It has declined in England and in Oxfordshire due to drainage and shading by woody growth. Druce (1917) listed Spartum Fen and Chipping Norton, Killick et al. (1998) recorded two sites both near Cottisford. During 2000-22 also at two sites - Cottisford and on the Thames by Oxford.
#### Conservaton work
Spartum Fen ( where it was nown in 1902) may become suitable following restoration work by the Freshwater Habitats Trust.
