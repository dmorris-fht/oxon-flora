### *Chamaemelum nobile* Chamomile 
(L.) All.
#### Ecology
It prefers slightly acid sometimes water-logged commons, but cannot tolerate the dense sward that follows loss of grazing.
#### Status & distribution
Confined to the south and west of the British Isles, Chamaemelum nobile was on the northern edge of its range in Oxfordshire, where this species had a scattered distribution.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed six sites, a maximum of eight sites have been reported, but none since 1983 at Nuney Green except R Comont's record from Bekynton House, Sutton Courtenay, which is likely to be planted.
#### Conservaton work
None.
