### *Potamogeton trichoides* Hairlike Pondweed 
Cham. & Schltdl.
#### Ecology
This is an annual which requires still or slowly moving water and tolerates raised nutrient levels. It flowers rarely, reproducing by turions (detachable buds) and colonizes new sites as it has at Otmoor.
#### Status & distribution
Rare in Scotland and Ireland, commonest in the east of England and Wales. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Only recently described and very similar to P. pusillus and P. berchtoldii. In 1968-98 at six tetrads. In 2000-22 at three sites in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
Appeared in scrapes made recently at Otmoor by the RSPB.
