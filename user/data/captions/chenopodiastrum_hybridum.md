### *Chenopodiastrum hybridum* Maple-leaved Goosefoot 
(L.) S.Fuentes, Uotila & Borsch
#### Ecology
This annual is usually a casual of deep, nutrient- and humus-rich, cultivated soil.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in southeastern England, and also in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at 13 sites, to Killick et al. (1998) at 23 tetrads, and during 2000-22 at 11 in Oxfordshire  and ten in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
