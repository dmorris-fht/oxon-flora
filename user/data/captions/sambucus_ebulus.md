### *Sambucus ebulus* Dwarf Elder 
L.
#### Ecology
Dwarf Elder forms robust perennial rhizomatous clumps in calcareous hedgerows, roadsides and rough ground with very high nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles except for the far north.  Mostly on the lime-rich soils in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Reported by Druce (1927) at 23 sites, and by Killick et al. (1998) at 27 in vc 23, and  2000-22 at eight in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
