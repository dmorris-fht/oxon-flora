### *Hypericum montanum* Pale St John's-wort 
L.
#### Ecology
This perennial herb grows on dry calcareous grassland or scrub with very low nutrient status.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in England and Wales, but absent from Ireland and nearly so from Scotland. Widespread in Oxfordshire, but mainly on the Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 19 sites in vc23 and Killick et al. (1998) 33 tetrads, and in 2000-22 in  seven localities in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
None.
