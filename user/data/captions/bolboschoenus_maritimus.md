### *Bolboschoenus maritimus* Sea Club-rush 
(L.) Palla
#### Ecology
In and by major rivers, in natural features on floodplains, and in gravel extractions in these areas. It prefers slightly alkaline and low nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Coastal throughout the British Isles and inland along major rivers. Known from 1897 to 1967 at Marcham Salt Spring LWS in vc22. First recorded in vc 23 in 2018.
#### Trends
Known from 1897 till 1967 at Marcham Salt Spring LWS in vc 22; appeared in 1978 a reservoir built in 1974 in Frilford. Appeared in vc 23 at Pinkhill, Farmoor in recent scrapes.
#### Conservaton work
Taken into cultivation from the Abingdon site to the Oxford Boatnic Garden in 2020. Favoured by the making of scrapes for wildfowl at Pinkhill and Otmoor.
