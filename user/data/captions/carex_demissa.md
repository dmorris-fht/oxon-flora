### *Carex demissa* Common Yellow-sedge 
Hornem.
#### Ecology
It mostly frequents acidic bogs and heaths with very low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widely distributed over the country, except the eastern Midlands.  Scattered in Oxfordshire
#### Trends
Lost from its less acidic sites. Druce (1927) listed 18 sites for C. flava, Killick et al. (1998) listed 13 tetrads, but many of these did not have suitable habitat.   During 2000-22 at four sites in Oxfordshire and three sites in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
Carex demissa appeared on Big Otmoor following the creation of scrapes by the RSPB.
