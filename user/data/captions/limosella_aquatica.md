### *Limosella aquatica* Mudwort 
L.
#### Ecology
This inconspicuous annual needs mud left exposed by falling water levels, and flourishes with slightly raised nutrients. It can become abundant on reservoir margins.
#### Status & distribution
It is an uncommon and erratic plant that is widely distributed in the British Isles and expanding in Scotland, especially in the central belt. In Oxfordshire mostly near the Thames in Oxford, and rare in the River Ray catchment.
#### Trends
Long known by the slow-moving Castle Mill Stream in Oxford, however following the high water levels of 2007-9 it disappeared there and re-appeared on Port Meadow where extensive mud areas had been created by soil anoxia as result of the same summer floods.
