### *Chenopodium vulvaria* Stinking Goosefoot 
L.
#### Ecology
This is an annual of highly enriched and high pH substrates on rubbish tips and waste places.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in the British isles, but mainly coastal in southeast England, Rare in Wales and Scotland, and absent from Ireland. Scattered in Oxfordshire, mostly around the city.
#### Trends
Stinking goosefoot has declined very dramatically and is now confined to three bird roosts on the coast (Preston et al. 2003). Last reported at Manor Rd., Oxford (SP5206) in 1937 by J P M Brenan, and Toot Baldon (5606) unconfirmed (Killick et al. 1998) .
