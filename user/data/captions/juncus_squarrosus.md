### *Juncus squarrosus* Heath Rush 
L.
#### Ecology
This small tufted wiry perennial flourishes in damp extremely acidic and extremely low nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Abundant in the north and west, this rush is also found on the wet heaths of the south and east. In Oxfordshire scattered on the Chiltern plateau and the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Known in the 18th C to Joseph Banks at Witney Common, and to John Sibthorp at Eynsham Heath, but "vainly sought" by Druce (1927). Reported from Kingwood Common LWS in 1987 and 2007.

None.
