### *Chrysosplenium alternifolium* Alternate-leaved Golden-saxifrage 
L.
#### Ecology
This small, creeping perennial favours slightly alkaline and moderatly nitrate-rich damp woodland.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles it is mainly in the north and west, usually with alkaline water, raised nutrients and bryophytes. In Oxfordshire only ever in the northwest of the county.
#### Trends
Only ever at Sarsgrove Wood SSSI.
#### Conservaton work
None.
