### *Daphne mezereum* Mezereon 
L.
#### Ecology
This small deciduous shrub is found in ancient and secondary, well-lit, woodlands and mature scrub on calcareous, and sometimes quite nutrient-rich, soils.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles its main centres of distribution are Lancashire, the southern Pennines and south-central counties. In Oxfordshire it is present moslty in the Chilterns.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) had 11 sites and Killick et al. (1998) 10 tetrads. During 2000-22 recorded at five sites in the Chilterns and one on the Corallian Ridge.
#### Conservaton work
All its curresnt sites are either SSSIs or LWSs. Protection from deer has been necessary at some sites.
