### *Spergularia rubra* Sand Spurrey 
(L.) J.Presl & C.Presl
#### Ecology
This is a prostrate annual or biennial of dry sandy or gravelly acidic soils. Very low nutrients and open ground are required for it to thrive. It is tolerant of trampling, and it forms colonies on paths and tracks.
#### Status & distribution
Widespead throughout the British Isles. In Oxfordshire mainly on the Chilterns plateau gravels and the Corallian Ridge sands.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) recorded ten sites in vc 23 and (Druce 1897) ten in vc22 Oxon. It was in nine tetrads in Killick et al. (1998). In 2000-22 at 11 sites in the county and seven in vc 23.
