### *Valeriana officinalis* Common Valerian 
L.
#### Ecology
This perennial marsh plant flourishes with more shading and higher nutrients than V. dioica.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread throughout the British Isles. In Oxfordshire moslty in the north of the county and rare in the Vale of White Horse.
#### Trends
Listed by Druce (1927) at 25 sites, and by Killick et al. (1998) at 227 tetrads in vc 23. During 2000-22 at c.184 sites.
#### Conservaton work
None.
