### *Galium tricornutum* Corn Cleavers 
Dandy
#### Ecology
This scrambling annual weed of arable requires calcareous and slightly raised nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles only ever common in the south and east of England. Widespread in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at 27 localities, but Killick et al. (1998) reported only seven and "probaly extinct".
#### Conservaton work
Introduced as seedlings raised from Rothampstead material by the Oxford Botanic Garden to Upper Seeds, Wytham, in 2013 and 2014. They grew and flowered but none presisted. In cultivation at College Lake, BBOWT reserve in Buckinghamshire.
