### *Comarum palustre* Marsh Cinquefoil 
L.
#### Ecology
This rhizomatous perennial of acidic bogs and fens is often grown as an ornamental and is thought not to be native in this county.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in the north and west  of the British Isles with outliers in Dorset and the Norfolk Broads. Probably introduced in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Introduced to Nuneham Courtenay arboretum (SU553986) from the Lake District by K J Burras. Reported from Cholsey Marsh in 1989 by J M Campbell, but the site is probably too alkaline for it to be native.
#### Conservaton work
None.
