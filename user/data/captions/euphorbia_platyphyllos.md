### *Euphorbia platyphyllos* Broad-leaved Spurge 
L.
#### Ecology
An annual spurge of calcareous mesotrophic soils, espeially arable field margins.
#### Status & distribution
In Britain it is mostly southeastern and appears to be spreading northwards.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) in the wild only at Tackley; 1900-1999 at six sites in vc 23 and one in vc 22.  During 2000-22 only at two sites, both in vc 23.
