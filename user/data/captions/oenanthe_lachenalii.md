### *Oenanthe lachenalii* Parsley Water-dropwort 
C.C.Gmel.
#### Ecology
This perennial grows in calcareous wetlands with raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
It grows around the coasts of the British Isles and scattered inland on calcareous wetlands. In Oxfordshire scattered in the clay vales and the Coralliasn Ridge.
#### Trends
In Oxfordshire it is a plant of alkaline fens, known to Druce (1927) at eight sites and to Killick et al. (1998) at ten tetrads in vc 23 , and in 2000-22 confined to six sites in Oxfordshire and two in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
At Cothill Fen SAC active management is being carried out by NE and BBOWT to control tall vegetation. Two of the six sites known in 2000-22 are SSSI where management was funded by an EU Wetland Environment Grant. Oxford City has funded work at Chilswell fen and The Freshwater Habitats Trust's Oxfordshire Fens Project has also been working at Hinksey Heights fen, at both of these sites O. lachenalii has been recently found following conservation work.
