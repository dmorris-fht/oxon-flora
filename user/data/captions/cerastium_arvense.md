### *Cerastium arvense* Field Mouse-ear 
L.
#### Ecology
This is a small perennial of slightly acidic and low nutrient dry grassland, banks, and heaths.
#### Status & distribution
Moslty in central, southern and eastern England, and eastern Scotland. Widespread and common in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 46 sites in vc 23. Eighty eight tetrads were recorded in Killick et al. (1998). Crawley (2005) reported eight sites in vc 22 Oxon. But only 22 sites have been noted in 2000-22.
