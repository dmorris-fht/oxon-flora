### *Silene conica* Sand Catchfly 
L.
#### Ecology
This small annual of nutrient-poor acid sandy soils grows among very short grass with bare patches. Numbers vary, and are best after previous hot summers.
#### Status & distribution
With a stronghold in the Breckland, it was rare in Scotland, Wales and elsewhere in England, but absent from Ireland. In Oxfordshire almost entirely confined to the Corallian ridge.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported three sites in vc23 and two in vc22 Oxon. The numbers on Frilford setside found by H J Killick ranged from 100 in 1997 to 300 in 2000, to three in 2010 and none on more recent excursions.
#### Conservaton work
Attempts to scarify around its last known site on the golf course practice gruond were not successful.
