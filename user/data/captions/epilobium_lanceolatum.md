### *Epilobium lanceolatum* Spear-leaved Willowherb 
Sebast. & Mauri
#### Ecology
A perennial herb of dry habitats, waste places and gardens; nearly all H J Killick’s records in southern England and southern Wales were by railway tracks. It prefers neutral conditions with raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mainly in the southwest of England, and to a lesser extent in southern England. In Oxfordshire mostly reported from the Cotswolds, the Corallian Ridge and in the Chilterns, but probably over-recorded.
#### Trends
None recorded in our area by Druce (1927). Four possible records from the 1980s. In 2000-22 possibly at one site.
