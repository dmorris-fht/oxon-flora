### *Arabis hirsuta* Hairy Rock-cress 
(L.) Scop.
#### Ecology
This is a perennial of calcareous pastures with low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread throughout the British Isles. Widespread in Oxfordshire except the southwest of the county.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 17 sites and Killick at al. (1998) 35 tetrads in vc 23. During 2000-22 in five localities, all in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
