### *Potamogeton polygonifolius* Bog Pondweed 
Pourr.
#### Ecology
This rhizomatous perennial flourishes in shallow, acidic and very low nutrient waters.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, except central southern England. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed seven sites and Killick et al. (1998) reported two tetrads, during 2000-22 known at one locality in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
