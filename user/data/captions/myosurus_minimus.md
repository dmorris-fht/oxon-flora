### *Myosurus minimus* Mousetail 
L.
#### Ecology
This is a small annual of seasonally flooded nutrient rich soils. It flourishes in disturbed areas especially where grazing animals drink.
#### Status & distribution
A plant of the south and east of England. Widespread in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Known to Druce in 1927 at seven sites, and to Killick et al. (1998) at 12 tetrads in vc 23; during 2000-22 at 13 localities in Oxfordshire. Losses are attributed to better drainage and infilling of ponds, but it is also a poor competitor so does not survive where no poached areas are available.
