### *Gentianella campestris* Field Gentian 
(L.) Börner
#### Ecology
This is a small (to 30 cm) biennial, or occasionally annual, of open, dry, neutral and infertile grassland and heaths. It requires short turf, with at least microhabitat disturbance (Pearman 2020).
#### Status & distribution
Mostly a northern plant, with outposts in the New Forest.
#### Trends
Three localities were reported by Druce (1927).  None since.
