### *Silene flos-cuculi* Ragged-Robin 
(L.) Clairv.
#### Ecology
This perennial of marshes and wet grassland flourishes in neutral soils and with slightly raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles except at high altitude. Widespread in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927 p.68) “Too common . . to need a list of localities." Killick et al. (1998) reported 282 tetrads. During 2000-22 in c.236 sites.
#### Conservaton work
Current work by the Freshwater Habitats Trust to open up calcareous fens will benfit this species.
