### *Agrostis vinealis* Brown Bent 
Schreb.
#### Ecology
This rhizomatous grass flourishes in dry acidic sandy grasslands with very low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles, but most frequent in coastal areas and the north and west. In Oxfordshire mostly on the Cotswolds, Corallian Ridge and Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
A total of ten localities had been recorded in Oxfordshire pre 2000., but taxonomic confusion obscures earlier finds. Three new sites have been added since 2000, suggesting it is under-recorded. Druce's specimens should be re-examined.
