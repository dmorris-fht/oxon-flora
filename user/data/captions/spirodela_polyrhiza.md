### *Spirodela polyrhiza* Greater Duckweed 
(L.) Schleid.
#### Ecology
This is a floating aquatic mostly in slightly calcareous, nutrient-rich, near-stagnant water.
#### Status & distribution
It is mostly south of Liverpool – Lincoln, with a gap in our area, and much decreased in the Midlands. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 20 localities. It was in 13 tetrads in Killick at al. (1998) and 2000-22 in four localities in Oxfordshire and three in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
