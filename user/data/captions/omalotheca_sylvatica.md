### *Omalotheca sylvatica* Heath Cudweed 
(L.) Sch.Bip. & F.W.Schultz
#### Ecology
This short lived perennial requires open, heathy woods or grassland with acidic soil and low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in Scotland and England. In Oxfordshire mainly on the Corallian Ridge and the Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 22 localities and Killick et al. (1998) 13 tetrads; during 2000-22 at four sites.
#### Conservaton work
Seed collected on Boars Hill in 2023.
