### *Euphrasia arctica* Arctic Eyebright 
Lange ex Rostrup
#### Ecology
A small annual hemiparasite of short grassland, favouring slightly alkaline conditions with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Abundant in Scotland, Wales and Ireland, Oxfordshire is on the edge of its range in England. In Oxfordshire mostly in the north and west of the county.
#### Trends
Of the five records in the county, the only one since 2000 is at Grim'sdyke Farm, Wootton.
