### *Muscari neglectum* Grape-hyacinth 
Guss. ex Ten.
#### Ecology
A bulbous perennial, often cultivated as an ornamental.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in England, scattered in Scotland, Ireland and Wales, but is considered native only in East Anglia.
#### Trends
It was known to Druce at Chadlington and used to be abundant in allotments, also in fields near the village.
#### Conservaton work
Frequent in the allotments at Chadlington, large numbers were moved to a holding area during development and returned to the public area afterwards. Monitored by the Wychwood Flora Group (formerly the Cotswold Rare Plants Group).
