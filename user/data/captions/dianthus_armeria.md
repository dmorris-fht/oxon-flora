### *Dianthus armeria* Deptford Pink 
L.
#### Ecology
This delicate small annual or biennial prefers neutral and low nutrient conditions. Short sward and open disturbed soil is also important.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in England, most frequent in the south. Rare in Scotland, Wales and Ireland. In Oxfordshire mostly in the Chilterns, but also on the Corallian Ridge and in the northwest of the county.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) reported seven sites and Killick et al. (1998) listed three tetrads, all are now apparently lost.
