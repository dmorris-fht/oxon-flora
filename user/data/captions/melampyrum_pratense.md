### *Melampyrum pratense* Common Cow-wheat 
L.
#### Ecology
A hemiparasitic annual of neutral to acidic woodlands with low nutrient soils.
#### Status & distribution
Widley common in the British Isles except the southeast Midlands of England. In Oxfordshire mostly on the Chilterns plateau with one site on the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 21 sites and Killick et al (1998) 35 tetrads, During 2000-22 BSBI had 13 localities in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
None.
