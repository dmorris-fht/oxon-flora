### *Cynoglossum officinale* Hound's-tongue 
L.
#### Ecology
This downy grey, unpalatable biennial prefers calcareous, often sandy, open ground and tolerates raised nutrients; it is often associated with rabbit warrens.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in southern England and Wales, and coastal further north. In Oxfordshire mainly on the Cotswolds, Corallian Ridge and the Chilterns.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) cited 40 localities, Killick et al. (1998) only 33 tetrads in vc 23, and during 2000-22 at 35 localities.
