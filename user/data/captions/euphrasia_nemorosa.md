### *Euphrasia nemorosa* Common Eyebright 
(Pers.) Wallr.
#### Ecology
An annual hemiparasite of short grassland, preferring slightly alkaline and moderate nitrate conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Common in the British Isles except the Scottish Highlands. Widespread in Oxfordshire but mostly on the Chilterns, scattered in the Cotswolds and rare on the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) (including E. curta) listed 30 sites and Killick et al. (1998) 97 tetrads.  During 2000-22 at 28 sites.
#### Conservaton work
None.
