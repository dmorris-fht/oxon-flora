### *Thesium humifusum* Bastard-toadflax 
DC.
#### Ecology
This perennial of heavily grazed chalk grassland is a hemiparasite on bedstraws (Wikipedia). It is found mainly on very low nutrient soils.
#### Status & distribution
Restricted to the chalk and limestone of southern England. In Oxfordshire on the Wessex Downs and to a lesser extent on the Chilterns and Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
It declined with the decline of sheep grazing, and ploughing of down land especially in East Anglia. Druce and Killick both listed five sites and in 2000-22  nine in Oxfordshire and three in vc23 and six in vc22.

None.
