### *Myosotis secunda* Creeping Forget-me-not 
Al.Murray
#### Ecology
This annual to perennial plant requires acidic, wet places with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Abundant in the north and west but absent from the Oxfordshire part of central England.
#### Trends
Druce had no records and of the eight records in KIllick et al. (1998), Otmoor  in 1977, is the most believable.
#### Conservaton work
None.
