### *Callitriche obtusangula* Blunt-fruited Water-starwort 
Le Gall
#### Ecology
A perennial aquatic of mesotrophic to eutrophic rivers, ponds, ditches and streams, also terrestrial on wet mud as water levels drop; preferring alkaline and nutrient enriched conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Mainly in central and southern England, usually coastal in Wales, rare in Scotland. In Oxfordshire mainly in the clay vales and rare on the Cotswolds and Chilterns.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 25 localities and Killick et al. (1998) 28 tetrads. During 2000-22 at six localities, all in vc 23.
