### *Plantago media* Hoary Plantain 
L.
#### Ecology
A perennial of lime-rich grassland with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Rare in Scotland, Ireland and Wales, and common in England except for Devon and Cornwall. In Oxfordshie abundant on the Chilterns and Cotswolds, and least common in the clay vale in the south west of the county.
#### Trends
Druce reported it as locally abundant. Killick et al. reported 214 tetrads.
#### Conservaton work
None.
