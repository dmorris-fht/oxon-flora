### *Triglochin palustris* Marsh Arrowgrass 
L.
#### Ecology
This perennial monocot requires marshy open conditions, tolerating some lime, but very low nitrates. It can survive under close mowing and grazing, but is difficult to find unless flowering.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles. In Oxfordshire it is widespread but absent from the Chilterns escarpment and Wessex Downs.
#### Trends
This species has declined in central England and especially the southeast.  Druce reported 36 sites and Killick et al. 42, found in 16 localities in 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
None.
