### *Lysimachia minima* Chaffweed 
(L.) U.Manns & Anderb.
#### Ecology
This tiny annual is largely coastal, growing on open, damp, sandy, acidic, and low nutrient soils.
#### Status & distribution
A cosmopolitan species; in England mostly in the south, in Scotland and Ireland in the west, and scattered in Wales and rare in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce only recorded it at Binfield OXF. Killick et al. (1998) point out that most of the 1968-98 records are likely to have been errors due to the similar names on recording cards. In 1946 at Bladon Heath J P M Brennan OXF and 1947 at Frilford Heath by E W Jones.
