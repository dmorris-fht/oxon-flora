### *Jasione montana* Sheep's-bit 
L.
#### Ecology
This sprawling biennial grows on heathland with acidic, very low nutrient, well-drained soils,
#### Status & distribution
Common in Cornwal, Wales, western north Enland and southern Scotland, and coastal in Ireland, scattered elsewhere. It is commoner in the west than the east of the county,
#### Trends
With the loss of lowland heath there has been a big decline both regionally and locally. Heathland is now a very rare habitat in Oxfordshire. Druce (1927) cited only eight localities. Killick et al. (1998) reported the last record was from Bayswater in 1940s.
