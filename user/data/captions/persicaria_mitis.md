### *Persicaria mitis* Tasteless Water-pepper 
(Schrank) Assenov
#### Ecology
This annual favours open draw-down zones with neutral pH and extremely high nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in the British Isles except northern Scotland. In Oxfordshie widely scattered but mostly in the clay vales.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 11 sites and Killick et al. (1998) 25 tetrads in vc 23. In 2000-22 at seven sites in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
None.
