### *Callitriche brutia subsp. hamulata* NA 
(Kütz. ex W.D.J.Koch) O.Bolòs & Vigo
#### Ecology
This is an aquatic of acidic and nutrient poor water.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread throughout the British Isles. Widespread in Oxfordshire but less common in the southwest of the county.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported it at 18 sites scattered over the county. Killick et al. (1998) doubted many of the 25 tetrads recoded in vc23. During 2000-22 confirmed widely at Otmoor.
#### Conservaton work
At Otmoor C. brutia ssp. hamulata has benefited greatly from the creation of many scrapes by the RSPB, and at the new Pill, a scrape made by the EA.
