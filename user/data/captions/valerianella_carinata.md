### *Valerianella carinata* Keeled-fruited Cornsalad 
Loisel.
#### Ecology
A winter annual of artificial habitats such as paths, paving, walls and gardens.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in south and central England. In Oxfordshie moslty on the Corallian Ridge and to a lesser extent on the Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
Reported by Druce (1927) at six sites and Killick et al. (1998) at five tetrads. During 2000-22 at c. 61 locations in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
