### *Eriophorum latifolium* Broad-leaved Cottongrass 
Hoppe
#### Ecology
This is a perennial of calcareous fens and marshes, requiring open conditions and very low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mainly found in Scotland, southern Wales, Ireland and northern England, but scattered in southern England. In Oxfordshire confined to the calcareous fens.
#### Trends
Druce  (1927) listed three sites, all now lost; also lost from Spartum Fen. Surviving at three of its seven localities in Oxfordshire. Sutton, Limb Brook and Stow Wood need searching.
#### Conservaton work
Extensive work has been carried out at all its surviving sites to retain short sward vegetation. Unfortunately fertilizer use adjacent to Cothill Fen may jeopardize its future there.
