### *Fragaria vesca* Wild Strawberry 
L.
#### Ecology
This creeping perennial grows in shade on neutal and moderately nutrient-rich soils.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles except the very north.
#### Trends
Widepread in Oxfordshire with concentrations on the Corallian and especially the Chiltern hills.
#### Conservaton work
None.
