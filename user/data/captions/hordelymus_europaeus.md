### *Hordelymus europaeus* Wood Barley 
(L.) Jess. ex Harz
#### Ecology
This grass is an Ancient Woodland Indicator which requires some lime and flourishes with high nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in England except in the west; it has a stronghold in the Chilterns.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at 18 sites and to Killick et al. (1998) at 44 tetrads, and during 2000-22 about 40 tetrads have been reported.
#### Conservaton work
None.
