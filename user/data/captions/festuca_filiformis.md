### *Festuca filiformis* Fine-leaved Sheep's-fescue 
Pourr.
#### Ecology
This very fine-leaved fescue is restricted to acidic and very low nutrient soils, mostly on open moorland in the north.
#### Status & distribution
Frequent in Scotland and widespread in England, though rare in Wales and southern Ireland. In Oxfordshire mostly on the Corallian Ridge and the Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed six sites in vc 23 and Killick et al. (1998) 12 tetrads. Reported from a total of 18 sites in vc 23 and seven in vc 22 Oxon. In 2000-22 only in seven sites in Oxfordshire and four in vc 23.
