### *Ervum gracile* Slender Tare 
DC.
#### Ecology
This delicate annual prefers slightly calcareous rides, grassy banks and arable on winter-wet clays that are baked hard in summer; it tolerates raised nitrate levels
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in southern and southeast England. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed seven sites and Killick et al. (1998) two tetrads in vc 23. During 2000-22 at five locations in the county and four in vc 23..
#### Conservaton work
Maintained at Upper Seeds, Wytham, by cultivation of a wartime field.
#### Variation
Reported by Druce as V. tetrasperma var. tenuissima and Vicia gracilis Lois
