### *Ranunculus peltatus* Pond Water-crowfoot 
Schrank
#### Ecology
Typically in ponds but also in the upper reaches of calcareous streams, it tolerates raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, except the extreme north and northwest.
#### Trends
Druce included some stream plants now placed in R. penicillatus, so he regarded it as common. Of 40 widespread tetrad records in Killick et al. (1998), only three were after 1986. In 2000-22 recorded at eight localities in the county.
