### *Lathyrus aphaca* Yellow Vetchling 
L.
#### Ecology
This distinctive annual is often an arable introduction, prefering dry, calcareous and moderate nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in the British Isles, mostly in the south. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 17 sites but said “Now almost extinct in Oxfordshire.” With us it is probably a casual, it was recorded in four tetrads by Killick et al. (1998). During 2000-22 only reported at the Slade Camp, Shotover in 2006.
