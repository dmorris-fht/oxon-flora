### *Nardus stricta* Mat-grass 
L.
#### Ecology
This low-growing grass thrives with light grazing and acidic, very low-nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Abundant in the north and west of the British Isles, but rare in the southern Midlands of England.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) reported seven sites in vc 23, Killick at al. (1998) had only two tetrads. None in Oxfordshire during 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
None.
