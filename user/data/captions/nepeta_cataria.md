### *Nepeta cataria* Cat-mint 
L.
#### Ecology
This perennial of waysides, hedges and arable field margins is usually on dry calcareous soils with raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widely scattered in the British Isles, but mostly in the southeast of England. Scattered in Oxfordshire, but mainly on the Chilterns plateau and the Cotswolds.
#### Trends
Reported by Druce (1927) at 39 sites and by Killick et al. (1998) at 30 tetrads in vc 23.  During 2000-22 at 22 sites in Oxfordshire and 16 in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
