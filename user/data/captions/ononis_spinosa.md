### *Ononis spinosa* Spiny Restharrow 
L.
#### Ecology
This species favours infertile calcareous grassland.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in central and southern England, often coastal. In Oxfordshire widespread, with many sites on the Wessex Downs, but least common in the mostly arable clay vale of the southwest.
#### Trends
Druce’s records are somewhat problematical in that there was a marked increase between 1886 and 1927, possibly due to the inclusion of some very spiny variants of O. repens.  Killick et al. (1998) cited 40 localities in vc23 and c.48  records in 2000-22  in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
None.
