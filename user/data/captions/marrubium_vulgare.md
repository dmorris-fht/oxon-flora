### *Marrubium vulgare* White Horehound 
L.
#### Ecology
A perennial of open conditions with alkaline soils and very high nutrient levels.
#### Status & distribution
Only native in coastal areas. Long cultivated, only ever a casual. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 15 sites, Killick et al (1998) only two. One (new, The Trapgrounds, Oxford) in 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
None.
