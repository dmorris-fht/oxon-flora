### *Diplotaxis tenuifolia* Perennial Wall-rocket 
(L.) DC.
#### Ecology
This is a perennial of dry banks and waste ground, preferring slightly alkaline and high nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, but still centred on ports and industrial areas. A scattered casual in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported it at Botley and Wolvercote, and Killick et al. (1998) listed eight sites in vc 23, at none of which has it been reported again; Bowen (1968) had no records in vc 22 Oxon. In 2000-22 BSBI recorded five sites in the county, suggesting it is a casual that is nearly holding its own.
