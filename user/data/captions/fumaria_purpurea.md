### *Fumaria purpurea* Purple Ramping-fumitory 
Pugsley
#### Ecology
An annual  of arable and waste land, preferring neutral soils with slightly raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
In Britain it is local, mostly in the north and near the west coast; Oxfordshire is well to the east of its main range.  Scattered in the county, but mainly in the clay vales.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed only Cowley, and Killick et al. (1998) only at Sturdy's Castle. None since 2000 (the record for City Farm is an error for F. capreolata)
