### *Epilobium palustre* Marsh Willowherb 
L.
#### Ecology
This delicate stoloniferous perennial grows in damp, slightly acid and low nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
It is common throughout the British Isles except in central England. Widespread in Oxfordshire though mainly reported from the Cotswolds, the Corallian Ridge and the Chilterns.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) reported 13 sites, Killick et al. (1998) had 39 tetrads (probably reflecting over-recording). During 2000-11 at 11 sites.
#### Conservaton work
Extensive management at Parsonage Moor should benefit this species.
