### *Trifolium striatum* Knotted Clover 
L.
#### Ecology
This is a small annual of thin sward on neutral to acidic soils with extremely low nutirents.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in England, but mostly coastal in Wales, Scotland and Ireland. Widespread in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
It has declined nationally and locally due to the abandonment of poor quality pasture land, which has then scrubbed over or been developed. Druce (1927) had nine sites and Killick et al. (1998) 19 tetrads in vc 23. During 2000-22 at 17 localities.

None.
