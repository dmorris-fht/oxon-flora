### *Bupleurum rotundifolium* Thorow-wax 
L.
#### Ecology
This annual was formerly a weed of arable land. It tends to grow on soils with very high pH and raised nutrient levels.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered, moslty In southern England, rare in Wales and Scotland and absent from Ireland. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) listed c. 25 sites, Killick et al. (1998) cited only two. Now found in bird-food seed-mixes.
