### *Carex vesicaria* Bladder-sedge 
L.
#### Ecology
Mostly in damp meadows with slightly acidic and somewhat raised nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, and also in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) reported 27 localities, Killick et al. (1998) had only 16 tetrads, but there are about 18  records in 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
Introduced at Milham Ford Park, Oxford.
