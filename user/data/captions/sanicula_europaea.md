### *Sanicula europaea* Sanicle 
L.
#### Ecology
This woodland floor perennial prefers slightly alkaline soils with raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles except at higher altitudes and in the Fens, perhaps reflecting an absence of woodland in these areas. In Oxfordshire mostly in the north and on the Chilterns.
#### Trends
Druce reported it as "Abundant . .  even in Oxford municipality." Killick et al. (1998) reported 224 tetrads. In 2000-22 frequent.
#### Conservaton work
None.
