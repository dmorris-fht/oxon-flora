### *Hottonia palustris* Water-violet 
L.
#### Ecology
This decorative perennial aquatic does not tolerate much disturbance or high eutrophication. It is unable to set seed from flowers of the same clone, requiring the different flower type (thrum or pin).
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in central, southern and eastern England, and rare in the rest of the Brisiths Isles. In Oxfordshire mostly on the Corallian Ridge and the River Ray catchment.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 29 sites, and Killick et al. (1998) had 22 tetrads, and in 2000-22 about 14 localities have been reported in the county..
#### Conservaton work
Introduced by Freshwater Habitats Trust to Pinkhill Meadow (Thames Water) (source Fowls Pill, Otmoor), on  6/12/17. Present every year up to last survey in August 2022. Survived in some but not all pools. Plants cover c 20 m area in 2022
