### *Hydrocotyle vulgaris* Marsh Pennywort 
L.
#### Ecology
This small perennial favours fens, marshes and wet meadows, usually with acidic pH and extremely low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in most of the British Isles except south-central England. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 23 sites, Killick et al. (1998) ten tetrads, and there were 12  localities during 2000-22 in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
Almost all its current sites have some conservation status.
