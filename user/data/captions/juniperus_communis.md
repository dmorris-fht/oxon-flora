### *Juniperus communis* Common Juniper 
L.
#### Ecology
This dioecious evergreen shrub has a limited life-span and often fails to establish from seed. It grows on a range of soil types, usually with low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
It is widely distributed in the British Isles; but in the south and east it is mostly confined to calcareous grasslands.
#### Trends
Reported by Druce at eight sites in the Cotswolds, including one bush at Juniper Hill and 21 in the Chilterns. Killick et al. (1998) reported three on the limestone and 20 on the Chilterns. In 2000-22 at 12 sites in the Chilterns, and three on the Wessex Downs where is was widespread in the 19th century.
#### Conservaton work
Planted on the M40 cutting at Aston Rowant. In 2021 Plantlife made exensive scrapes at Aston Upthorpe SSSI.
