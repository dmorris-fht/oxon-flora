### *Ophrys sphegodes* Early Spider-orchid 
Mill.
#### Ecology
A short-lived winter-green tuberous perennial of tightly grazed, low nutrient, calcareous grassland.
#### Status & distribution
On the chalk of the south coast of England, rare inland. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed nine sites, mostly recently in 1920. Killick et al.(1998) cited one at Kirtlington Quarry in 1975. Not seen since.
#### Conservaton work
None.
