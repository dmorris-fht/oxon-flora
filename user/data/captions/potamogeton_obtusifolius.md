### *Potamogeton obtusifolius* Blunt-leaved Pondweed 
Mert. & W.D.J.Koch
#### Ecology
This aquati perennial is found in neutral, mesotrophic, still or slowly flowing water.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles.
#### Trends
Not known to Druce. Killick et al. (1998) had seven sites. It appeared in 1994 in newly dug scrapes at Pinkhill, between the Farmoor reservoir and the Thames, and is now abundant there.
