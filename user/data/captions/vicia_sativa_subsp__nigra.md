### *Vicia sativa subsp. nigra* Narrow-leaved Vetch 
(L.) Ehrh.
#### Ecology
This scrambling annual favours rather bare ground usually on poor sandy soils.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles but declining in the Midlands. Scattered in Oxfordshire but most on the Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed five sites for V. angustifolia, and Killick et al. (1998) had five tetrads, and during 2000-22 c.30 in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
