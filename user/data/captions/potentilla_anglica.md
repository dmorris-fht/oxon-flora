### *Potentilla anglica* Trailing Tormentil 
Laichard.
#### Ecology
A trailing perennial found on sandy neutral to acidic soils with raised nutrients;  frequenting woodland edges and rides.
#### Status & distribution
It is common in west and southwest England, Wales and Ireland. In Oxfordshire mostly on the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 18 sites (as P. procumbens), Killick et al. quoted 25 tetrads in vc 23, and in 2000-22 at twelve localities in the county.

None.
