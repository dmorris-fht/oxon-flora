### *Fumaria vaillantii* Few-flowered Fumitory 
Loisel.
#### Ecology
It grows almost always in arable land on chalk with slightly raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
In Britain mostly in southeast England. Oxfordshire is at the northwesten edge of its range; it is found on lime-rich soils, mostly on the Chilterns plateau
#### Trends
Druce had five sites and Killick et al. (1998) 22. During 2000-22 at four sites.

None.
