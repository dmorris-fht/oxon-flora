### *Trifolium scabrum* Rough Clover 
L.
#### Ecology
This small annual requires nutrient poor dry, sandy but calcareous soils with very short, open turf. Druce reported seven sites, mostly around Oxford city. Most recently at the Bretch, but not refound. Lost from half its vc22 sites.
#### Status & distribution
Coastal on the south of England, Wales and eastern Ireland and on the east coast of Scotland north to Inverness, scattered inland.  Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Formerly found on inland sites where it has declined greatly. Druce (1927) reported seven sites in vc 23, and Killick et al. (1998) four tetrads, but now absent from vc 23. Historically at four sites in vc 22 Oxon and during 2000-22 at two.

None.
