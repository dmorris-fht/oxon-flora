### *Valerianella dentata* Narrow-fruited Cornsalad 
(L.) Pollich
#### Ecology
This small annual grows on chalky arable soils with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
This species is mostly found on the chalk and limestone in southern and eastern England; rare in Scotland and Ireland. In Oxfordshire on lime-rich soils.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 35 sites and Killick et al. (1998) 25 tetrads and in 2000-22 at c. 18 sites in the county.
#### Conservaton work
Its survival may be due to its relatively long seed–life, and the increase in arable headlands.
