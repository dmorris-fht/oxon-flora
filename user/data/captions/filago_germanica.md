### *Filago germanica* Common Cudweed 
L., non Huds.
#### Ecology
This small annual of dry, open ground prefers slightly alkaline soils with moderate nutrients. Formerly an arable weed, it is a poor competitor, and does not respond well to nutrient enrichment (Fichte K & Schultz 1992).
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, but commonest in eastern, central and southern England. Widespread in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 36 localities and Killick et al. (1998) 12 tetrads in vc 23, while the BSBI database has 31 localities during the Killick et al. time period (1968-1997); and during 2000-22 listed 41 in Oxfordshire and 31 in vc23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
