### *Ulex gallii* Western Gorse 
Planch.
#### Ecology
This small shrub flourishes on somewhat acidic and very infertile soils.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in western England, and Ireland except the central counties. Also scattered in Scotland. In Oxfordshire mostly restricted to the northwest of the county.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed five sites, and Killick et al. (1998) one; during 2000-22 two plants known at Tadmarton golf course and one at North Leigh Common.
#### Conservaton work
None.
