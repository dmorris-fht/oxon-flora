### *Potamogeton coloratus* Fen Pondweed 
Hornem.
#### Ecology
This aquatic perennial prefers very high alkalinity and raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles its main strongholds are Norfolk, Cambridgeshire, Anglesey, the Hebrides and Central Ireland. In Oxfordshire only in fens in the Cothill area on the Corallian limestone.
#### Trends
Still present at Cothill at the SAC where it was known to Druce (1927), and refound at Dry Sandford Pit SSSI. Also at Turf Pits Covert and found in 2023 at Gainfield.
#### Conservaton work
BBOWT has be very active at Parsonage Moor and The Freshwater Habitats Trust used an EU Wetland Environment Grant to carry out restoration at Barrow Farm Fen SSSI.
