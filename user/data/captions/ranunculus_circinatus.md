### *Ranunculus circinatus* Fan-leaved Water-crowfoot 
Sibth.
#### Ecology
This aquatic grows in deep (1-3 m), clear, base-rich, high nutrient, mostly still, water.
#### Status & distribution
Widely distributed in the British Isles, except northern Scotland. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 39 sites, and Killick et al. (1998) at 19 tetrads in vc 23, and during 2000-22 it has been recorded at nine localities in the county.
