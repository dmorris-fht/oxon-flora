### *Mentha arvensis* Corn Mint 
L.
#### Ecology
A perennial of grassy conditions with alkaline soils and high nutrient levels.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles commonest in the south. Widespread in Oxfordshire, but less in the clay vales and the Wessex Downs.
#### Trends
While Druce listed  35 sites, Killick et al. (1998) had 252 tetrads in vc 23; and during 2000-22 BSBI recoded 109 localities in Oxfordshire and 108 in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
