### *Ornithopus perpusillus* Bird's-foot 
L.
#### Ecology
This is a tiny prostrate annual, with stems that can grow to 40 cm. It thrives on dry, nutrient poor sandy soils with short or patchy sward.
#### Status & distribution
Mainly in Wales and England, but  less common in Scotland and Ireland. In Oxfordshire mostly on lime-rich soils of the Chilterns, Corallian Ridge and Cotswolds.
#### Trends
Recorded by Druce in 10 localities, and by Killick et al. (1998) and in 2000-22 in nine tetrads in Oxfordshire and eight in vc23.
#### Conservaton work
None. Seed was collected from the Boars HIll population in 2003 and seedlings retained by C R Lambrick and given to Sue Helm.
