### *Ranunculus arvensis* Corn Buttercup 
L.
#### Ecology
This annual of arable fields grows on a range of soils with raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mainly in the English lowlands.  Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce 91927) said it was locally abundant and widely distributed, listing 43 sites. Killick et al. (1998) cited 50 tetrads. In 2000-22 only three likely localities.
#### Conservaton work
None.
