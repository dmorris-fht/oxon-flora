### *Silene gallica* Small-flowered Catchfly 
L.
#### Ecology
This winter annual  prefers coastal sites or dry sandy arable, grassland and wasteland with neutral soil, and it tolerates slightly raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly coastal in southern England and Wales. In Oxfordshire mainly on the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Nationally it has declined hugely. Druce (1927) gave two sites as alien. Killick et al. (1998) four tetrads. 2000-22 only Culham Field.
#### Conservaton work
None.
