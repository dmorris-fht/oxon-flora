### *Bidens cernua* Nodding Bur-marigold 
L.
#### Ecology
This waterside annual prefers neutral to alkaline pH and tolerates high nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, but commonest around London, in Norfolk and the northwest Midlands. It was widespread in Oxfordshire, but mainly along the river corridors.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 14 sites and Killick et al. (1998) reported 21 tetrads (11 north of Oxford); but Crawley (2005) none in vc 22 Oxon. During 2000-22 there have been seven records, all in vc 23.
