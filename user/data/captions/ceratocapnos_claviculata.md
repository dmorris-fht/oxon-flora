### *Ceratocapnos claviculata* Climbing Corydalis 
(L.) Lidén
#### Ecology
A delicate scrambling annual of shady sites on poor acid sandy soils where these factors deter competitors. It often appears after clearing, and can co-exist with bracken.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles, but absent from western Ireland; moslty in northern England, Scotland and Wales. In central and northwestern Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported four sites in vc 23, and Killick et al. (1998) eight. During 2000-22 at 15 sites in the county and nine in vc 23.
