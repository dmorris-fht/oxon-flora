### *Schoenoplectus tabernaemontani* Grey Club-rush 
(C.C.Gmel.) Palla
#### Ecology
This emergent wetland perennial is frequent in coastal marshes, often around gravel pits. It prefers margins of still or moving calcareous water and high nutrient levels.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in England and mainly coastal in Wales, Scotland and Ireland. Scattered in Oxfordshire, but mostly in the clay vales.
#### Trends
This species has increased recently, especially inland, Druce (1927) knew only the one site, and Killick et al. (1998) reported four tetrads in vc 23, and in 2000-22 c. 16 in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
None.
