### *Sonchus palustris* Marsh Sow-thistle 
L.
#### Ecology
A tall perennial of marshy ground with slightly raised alkaline conditions and very high nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly between Peterborough and Cambridge, and in the East Anglian Broads
#### Trends
Only ever at one site near Stanton St John, gone in 1923.
#### Conservaton work
None
