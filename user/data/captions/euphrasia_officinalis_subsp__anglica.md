### *Euphrasia officinalis subsp. anglica* Eyebright 
(Pugsley) Silverside
#### Ecology
A small annual hemiparasite of dry grassland, preferring neutral soils with low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Commonest in Wales, the southwest and southern England. In Oxfordshire, mostly in the northeast.
#### Trends
Reported by W Frost in Oxford in 1945; by Henderson in Gaginwell in 1968; anon in SP52N in 1972;  by Robert Barber in Howe Wood in 2007. None confirmed.

None
