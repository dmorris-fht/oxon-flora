### *Potentilla erecta* Tormentil 
(L.) Raeusch.
#### Ecology
This low-growing creeping perennial favours heaths and dry grassland on very acidic and extremely low nutrient soils.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles except the Fens. Widespread in Oxfordshire except the Wessex Downs.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) noted it as locally common and widely distributed. Killick et al. (1998) gave 163 tetrads.
#### Conservaton work
None.
