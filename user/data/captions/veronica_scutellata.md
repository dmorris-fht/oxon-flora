### *Veronica scutellata* Marsh Speedwell 
L.
#### Ecology
This slender perennial of sunny wet places prefers low-nutrient and slightly acidic conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles. In Oxfordshire it is most frequent on the Corallian Ridge, but also present on the Chilterns and Cotswold plateaux.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) listed 20 sites, Killick et al. (1998) had 33 tetrad,s and during 2000-22 c. 23 sites were reported in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
