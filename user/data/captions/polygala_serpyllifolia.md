### *Polygala serpyllifolia* Heath Milkwort 
Hosé
#### Ecology
This is a small perennial herb of acidic, very low nutrient grasslands, moors and heaths.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the Brisih Isles, but rare in central eastern England. In Oxfordshire mostly on the Chilterns plateau, the Corallian Ridge, and scattered further north.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported five sites and Killick et al. (1998) 12 tetrads in vc 23. In Oxfordshire in 2000-22 at nine sites.
#### Conservaton work
A restoration programme by Shotover Wildlife
