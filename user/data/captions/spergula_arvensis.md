### *Spergula arvensis* Corn Spurrey 
L.
#### Ecology
This is an annual of open, disturbed, light, neutral soils with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles. Widespread in Oxfordshire with a concentration on the Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
Nationally there has been a steep decline, not replicated here -  while Druce (1927) reported 29 sites in vc23, and Killick et al. (1998) also found 29 tetrads, and during 2000-2022 c. 33 localities in the county and 31 in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
