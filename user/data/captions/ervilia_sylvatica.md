### *Ervilia sylvatica* Wood Vetch 
(L.) Schur
#### Ecology
This is a scrambling perennial of woods, wood margins and clearings, on slightly calcareous soils with raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles except East Anglia and western Ireland. Once widespread in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported nine sites and Killick et al. (1998) reported 19. None during 2000-22. The decline may reflect the cessation of coppicing; in Wiltshire Grose (1957) reported it ‘sometimes abundant in newly coppiced woods’ and A J Dunn found it following conifer clearance in 1988.
#### Conservaton work
None.
