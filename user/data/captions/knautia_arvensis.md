### *Knautia arvensis* Field Scabious 
(L.) Coult.
#### Ecology
This is a robust rosette forming perennial of calcareous grassland  with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles, except the north and west of Scotland. In Oxfordshire widespread but mostly on the chalk and limestone.
#### Trends
2000-22  c. 320 localities in Oxfordshire and 263 in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
