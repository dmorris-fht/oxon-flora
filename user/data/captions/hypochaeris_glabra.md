### *Hypochaeris glabra* Smooth Cat's-ear 
L.
#### Ecology
This is an annual of dry acidic grasslands with extremely low nutrients and short sward.
#### Status & distribution
In Great Britain its stronghold is in the Breckland of East Anglia, and it is also found scattered around the coast. In Oxfordshire probably confined to the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) noted three sites and Killick et al. (1998) none since 1923 at Stow Wood. During 2000-22 only at one site in vc 22 Oxon.
#### Conservaton work
Its current site is an SSSI where the mowing regime has been relaxed to enable this plant to set seed.
