### *Myriophyllum alterniflorum* Alternate Water-milfoil 
DC.
#### Ecology
This submerged aquatic flourishes in base-poor water.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the northern and western British Isles, but rare in the southern Midlands.
#### Trends
Not recorded by Druce from vc 23, but during 1968-98 from a total of four tetrads. Not found in Oxfordshire during 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
None.
