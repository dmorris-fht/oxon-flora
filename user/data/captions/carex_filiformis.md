### *Carex filiformis* Downy-fruited Sedge 
L.
#### Ecology
In calcium-rich, damp or dry grassland with low or raised nutrient levels. It can also be plentiful on woodland rides and road verges.
#### Status & distribution
It has a curious distribution, with a concentration around Cirencester, and very few sites further east. Also curiously distributed in Oxfordshire, at Westwell Gorse on the limstone, at Hartslock on the chalk, at Otmoor on slightly acidic clay, and at Grafton Lock in a Thames floodplain hay meadow.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) knew it from two sites, Killick et al. (1998) listed three tetrads, and during 2000-22 known at four loclities.
#### Conservaton work
R W David noticed that it was becoming overgrown at Westwell and annual monitoring and cutting has seen it restored from a few plants to thousands (Killick et al., 1998).
