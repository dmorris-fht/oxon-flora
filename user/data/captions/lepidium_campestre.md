### *Lepidium campestre* Field Pepperwort 
(L.) W.T.Aiton
#### Ecology
Annual, rarely biennial, of open or arable well-drained soils and walls, preferring neutral pH and slightly raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles, but mostly in the the central, south and eastern parts of England.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 27 sites, and Killick et al. (1998) 53 tetrads in vc 23. In 2000-22  at c.  21 localities in Oxfordshire and 12 in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
