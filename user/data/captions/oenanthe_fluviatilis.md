### *Oenanthe fluviatilis* River Water-dropwort 
(Bab.) Coleman
#### Ecology
This submerged and emergent aquatic perennial is found mostly in clear, alkaline, meso-eutrophic rivers and streams.
#### Status & distribution
In east and south England it is widespread, and in Oxfordshire is at the northwest edge of its range.
#### Trends
Druce described it in vc 23 as ‘common in the shallower parts of all the larger streams’, but Killick et al. (1998) reported it only in ten tetrads, and 2000-22 only reported it at three sites.
#### Conservaton work
It has declined owing to eutrophication, dredging, boat movements and canalization
