### *Trifolium fragiferum* Strawberry Clover 
L.
#### Ecology
This is a perennial of damp meadows on heavy soils which are alkaline and have raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mainly in southeast England, but also coastal in Ireland, Wales and Scotland as far north as the Outer Hebrides. Widespread in Oxfordshire except for the Wessex Downs and the Chilterns.
#### Trends
It has declined nationally, and here, through loss of old pastures. Druce (1927) listed 34 sites and Killick et al. (1998) reported 50 tetrads in cv 23. During 2000-2022 at 18 localities in Oxfordshire.

None.
