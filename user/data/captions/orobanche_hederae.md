### *Orobanche hederae* Ivy Broomrape 
Duby
#### Ecology
An annual or perennial parasite restricted to ivy, especially Hedera hibernica, but we also have it on H. helix.
#### Status & distribution
The native area, in southwest England, Wales and Ireland, mostly lies near the coast and where the February minimum for 1901-1930 was above 38 F, but the cold winter of 2010 has not reduced it here. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
In two tetrads in Killick et al. (1998) and seven sites during 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
The Abingdon colony has been known since 2002; it could have arrived when HJ Killick took home a specimen from Wolfson College.
