### *Polygonatum multiflorum* Solomon's-seal 
(L.) All.
#### Ecology
This elegant perennial is often planted, and can also be confused with the ornamental garden species ( P. x hybridum).
#### Status & distribution
It is native mostly in central southern England.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported five sites, Killick et al. (1998) 14 tetrads. During 2000-22 reported from thirteen sites, but most of these are probaly planted or misidentified.
#### Conservaton work
None.
