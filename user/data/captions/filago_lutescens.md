### *Filago lutescens* Red-tipped Cudweed 
Jord.
#### Ecology
This small winter or spring annual of dry open ground prefers slightly acidic and very low nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles very local in south eastern England. In Oxfordshire mostly on the Corallian Ridge and the Chiltern plateau.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed seven sites and Killick et al. (1998) only one. Known from five sites in vc22 Oxfordshire. Last record at Sonning Eye.
