### *Misopates orontium* Weasel's-snout 
(L.) Raf.
#### Ecology
This spring-germinating annual was formerly found in light, neutral to slightly acidic cultivated land. It prefers raised nutrients. Recently in old quarries and as a garden weed, perhaps susceptible to herbicides.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, but mostly in the south and coastal in Wales and East Anglia. Scattered in Oxfordshire, but mostly in the Chilterns.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at seven sites and to Killick et al. (1998) at 18 tetrads, but now only at four sites in Oxfordshire with the best population at the Reading Road allotments in Henley.
#### Conservaton work
Introduced at Sonning Eye Quarry in 2017. Promoted in Janet Welsh's garden, where it was when she came.
