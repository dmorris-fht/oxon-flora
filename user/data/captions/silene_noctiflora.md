### *Silene noctiflora* Night-flowering Catchfly 
L.
#### Ecology
An annual of cultivated land, germinating in spring; mainly on dry sandy or lime-rich soils with raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in southern and eastern England and Scotland. In Oxfordshire mostly in the north of the county.
#### Trends
Nationally it has declined hugely. Druce (1927) gave 32 sites in vc 23. Killick et al. (1998) 87 tetrads in vc 23. In 2000-22 in 21 tetrads.  In 2000-22 c. 25 localities in the county and in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
