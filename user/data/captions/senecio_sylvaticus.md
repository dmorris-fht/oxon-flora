### *Senecio sylvaticus* Heath Groundsel 
L.
#### Ecology
This is an erect (to 70 cm) hairy annual which favours non-calcareous sandy soils. The seed bank is long-lived.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles. Widespread in Oxfordshire with a concentration on the Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported more than 30 sites, saying it was “frequent on the sandy commons of the Chilterns". Killick et al. (1998) mapped 37 tetrads, and in 2000-22 c. 12 sites were reported.
#### Conservaton work
None.
