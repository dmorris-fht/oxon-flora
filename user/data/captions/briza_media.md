### *Briza media* Quaking-grass 
L.
#### Ecology
This delightful grass is most frequent in dry, low nutrient, calcareous grassland, but also occurs in damp places, and in acidic conditions in the north.
#### Status & distribution
Distributed throughout the Brisih Isles, except the western Highlands of Scotland, where it is rare. Widespread in Oxfordshire, but scattered in the clay vales.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) wrote it was "Locally abundant and widely but not generally distributed." Killick et al. (1998) reported 257 tetrads, and during 2000-22 c.260 were reported in the county.

None.
