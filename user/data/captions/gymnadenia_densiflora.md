### *Gymnadenia densiflora* Marsh Fragrant-orchid 
(Wahlenb.) A.Dietr.
#### Ecology
This is an orchid of base-rich fens, wet meadows and north-facing calcareous, low nutrient grasslands.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles.  Mostly in the Cothill area, but also at Woodsides Meadow in the River Ray  catchment.
#### Trends
Recently recognized as a distinct species.
#### Conservaton work
None.
