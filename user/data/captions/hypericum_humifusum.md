### *Hypericum humifusum* Trailing St John's-wort 
L.
#### Ecology
This short-lived, low-growing perennial is found along trackways and in other semi-shaded habitats; it favours acidic and leached soils.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, except the fens and the far north. Widespread in Oxfordshire, but mostly on the Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 27 sites, Killick et al. (1998) 36 tetrads; during 2000-22 at 23 localities in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
