### *Carduus tenuiflorus* Slender Thistle 
Curtis
#### Ecology
This annual or biennial is usually coastal, and inland occurences are as casuals.
#### Status & distribution
Inland occurences are scattered in England, and in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Always rare, Druce (1927) listed six sites, Killick et al.  (1998) listed three tetrads. and there were two finds during 2000-22, both in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
