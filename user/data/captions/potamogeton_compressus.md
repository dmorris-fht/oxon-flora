### *Potamogeton compressus* Grass-wrack Pondweed 
L.
#### Ecology
This aquatic perennial requires base rich, but not eutrophic, water.
#### Status & distribution
Always restricted to central and eastern England and Wales. In Oxfordshre mainly in the Thames and the Oxford canal.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at 18 sites, but to Killick et al. (1998) only at Kiln Lane Pit (= Henry Stephen C S Lewis BBOWT Reserve ) in 1979.
#### Conservaton work
None.
