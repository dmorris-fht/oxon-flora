### *Carex pallescens* Pale Sedge 
L.
#### Ecology
In open woods and hay meadows, mostly on neutral soils with raised nutrient levels.
#### Status & distribution
Widely distributed in the country, as also in the county.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) listed ten localities, Killick et al. (1998) had 26, and there were nine during 2000-22 of which eight are in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
