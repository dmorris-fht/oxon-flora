### *Logfia minima* Small Cudweed 
(Sm.) Dumort.
#### Ecology
Small Cudweed is a tiny annual of dry, open places on very infertile, acidic soils.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the Brisitsh Isles, and scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 11 sites in vc 23, but Killick et al. (1998) only three tetrads. In vc 22 Oxon a total of five sites have been reported. In 2000-22 from six localities in Oxfordshire and three in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
Cumnor Hurst is an SSSI and areas have been scarified for the benefit of Heather Calluna.
