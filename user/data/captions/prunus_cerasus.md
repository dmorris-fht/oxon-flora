### *Prunus cerasus* Dwarf Cherry 
L.
#### Ecology
This shrub or small tree
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce appears (like others subsequently) not to have distinguished this species sufficiently from P. avium and P. cerasifera. .“Prunu cer” on the Flora of Oxfordshire recording cards may have attracted many records belonging to the common P. cerasifera. Therefore HJK doubted many of the 113 tetrad records (p.162), almost none of which have been re-found. Moreover this species is often planted.

None.
