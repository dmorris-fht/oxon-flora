### *Medicago sativa subsp. falcata* Sickle Medick 
(L.) Arcang.
#### Ecology
This perennial prefers neutral, open sites with moderate nutrients; it is intolerant of grazing. In Britain it is native on grassy heaths and roadsides on sandy and calcareous soils in East Anglia and a scattered introduction elsewhere.
#### Status & distribution
In Britain it is native on grassy heaths and roadsides on sandy and calcareous soils in East Anglia and a scattered introduction elsewhere. Scattered in Wales and Scotland, mostly in eastern England. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed four sites as did Killick et al. (1998). During 2000-22 in two sites both in vc 23.
