### *Neotinea ustulata* Burnt Orchid 
(L.) R.M.Bateman, Pridgeon & M.W.Chase
#### Ecology
This small tuberous perennial prefers calcareous grassland with extremely low nitrogen levels. It flowers only sporadically, mostly in late May, though there is also a July-flowering form.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in England, with a stronghold in Wiltshire.
#### Trends
Known to Druce at ten sites, and to Killick et al. (1998) at 12 tetrads in vc 23; during 2000-22 only at Juniper Valley, Aston Upthorpe SSSI.

Monitoring.
