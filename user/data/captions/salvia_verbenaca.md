### *Salvia verbenaca* Wild Clary 
L.
#### Ecology
This aromatic perennial was often planted on graves, but is usually found on low nutrient waysides.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in the south, often coastal. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed c. 30 sites and Killick et al. (1998 p.211) had 19 tetrads, but it may have stabilized in Oxfordshire where seven of the 21 2000-22 sites are new, suggesting it naturalizes readily and/or was under-recorded. Crawley (2005) reported five sites in vc 22.
#### Conservaton work
None.
