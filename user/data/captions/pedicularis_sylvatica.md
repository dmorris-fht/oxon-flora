### *Pedicularis sylvatica* Lousewort 
L.
#### Ecology
This perennial hemi-parasite grows in acidic bogs with very low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Common in Scotland, Wales, Ireland and the north, south and west of England, but rare in central and eastern areas. In Oxfordshire on the Chilterns plateau, the Corallian Ridge and the Cotswolds.
#### Trends
Druce reported 25 sites and Killick et al. (1998) 11 tetrads, but  during 2000-22 only in three in Oxfordshire and two in vc 23 (counting the two parts of Cradle and Grounds Farms Banks as one).
