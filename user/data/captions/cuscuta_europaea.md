### *Cuscuta europaea* Greater Dodder 
L.
#### Ecology
This is an annual, rarely perennial, rootless parasite germinating primarily on common nettle, and spreading onto hop and other species, scrambling through scrub. In nutrient enriched, damp places by rivers and ditches,
#### Status & distribution
Curiously distributed along the chalk in south eastern England and around Bristol and in the Severn valley. In Oxfordshire widely distributed sites.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at 20 sites in vc 23. Recorded by Killick et al. (1998) at ten tetrads, and still at four sites in vc 22 Oxon (Crwaley (2005). During 2000-22  reported at nine  localities in Oxfordshire, and at five in vc 23.
