### *Carum carvi* Caraway 
L.
#### Ecology
A perennial naturalized or casual in arable land, meadows and waste ground, preferring slightly alkaline and raised nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Introduced to Britain by 1375. Now scattered over the British Isles with a hotspot in London; lost from many places in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
It is grown less than formerly, Druce (1927) reported 10 sites and Killick at al. (1998) two, but none have been reported in 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
None.
