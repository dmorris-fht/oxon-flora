### *Carex rostrata* Bottle Sedge 
Stokes
#### Ecology
It grows in peaty or boggy areas or edges of water bodies, with often acidic and very low nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles but scattered in the southern Midlands. Widespread in Oxfordshire but absent from the chalk.
#### Trends
Decreasing in central and southeast England and also in Oxfordshire. Druce (1927) listed nine sites, Killick et al. (1998) 15 tetrads, and during 2000-22 in nine sites in the county and in seven in vc 23 .
#### Conservaton work
This species is benefitting from scrub control at Cothill SAC, Lye Valley SSSI and Frilford SSSI.
