### *Wahlenbergia hederacea* Ivy-leaved Bellflower 
(L.) Rchb.
#### Ecology
This creeping perennial herb of damp, wet or boggy places on acid soils with low nutrients; it grows in pastures, heaths and shady woodland.
#### Status & distribution
Mainly in western Wales and the southwest of England. In Oxfordshire only from the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Druce listed two sites, Bagley Wood and Boars Hill (Wootton Heath).
#### Conservaton work
None.
