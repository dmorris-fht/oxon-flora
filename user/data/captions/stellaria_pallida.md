### *Stellaria pallida* Lesser Chickweed 
(Dumort.) Crép.
#### Ecology
This small inconspicuous annual is confined to dry neutral to acidic sandy soils in open conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Mainly in the east of England and coastal in Wales, and eastern in Scotland and Ireland.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 28 sites, but Killick et al. (1998) only one. In 2000-22 in 21 sites in Oxfordshire and eight in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
