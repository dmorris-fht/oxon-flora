### *Valerianella rimosa* Broad-fruited Cornsalad 
Bastard
#### Ecology
This winter annual grows in calcareous arable and quarries, and does not tolerate raised nutrient levels.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in southern England, rare in Ireland, Wales and northern England; absent from Scotland. In Oxordshire only on the lime-rich soils.
#### Trends
It has suffered a huge decline both nationally and in Oxfordshire. Druce (1927) reported five sites and Killick et al. (1998) seven tetrads in vc 23. During 2000-22 two localities in vc 23 and one in vc 22 Oxon but not refound on a second visit.
#### Conservaton work
None.
