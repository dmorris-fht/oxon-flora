### *Trifolium subterraneum* Subterranean Clover 
L.
#### Ecology
This winter annual forms early-flowering procumbent patches in short, sometimes close mown, turf on very infertile and rather acid, sandy or gravelly soil.
#### Status & distribution
In Great Britain it was mostly by the south and southeast coasts, with some post-1970 inland records especially in the Lincolnshire Wolds, Berkshire and the north Chilterns.
#### Trends
In vc 23 it was known to J Sibthorp between Shotover and Cuddesdon until 1907 and reported at Stow Wood by Boswell in 1860. In vc 22 Oxon in four localities in Bowen (1968) (Grove, Frilford, Kingston Bagpuize and Tubney) but only in two areas, all near Frilford, during 2000-22.
