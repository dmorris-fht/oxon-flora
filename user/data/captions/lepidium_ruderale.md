### *Lepidium ruderale* Narrow-leaved Pepperwort 
L.
#### Ecology
This annual is a casual coloniser of walls and rubbish tips, but especially bare ground by the sea. As a halophyte it has spread along salted roads. It is probably this which accounts for its appearance along the A420 at Tubney and Botley.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in the British Isles, but mainly in central and southeast England. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) and Killick et al. (1998) both reported it at four sites. During 2000-22 at three sites, two of them on the A420.
#### Conservaton work
None.
