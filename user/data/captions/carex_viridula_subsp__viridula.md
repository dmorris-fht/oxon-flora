### *Carex viridula subsp. viridula* Small-fruited Yellow-sedge 
Michx.
#### Ecology
Inland in England in wet, open meadows, pastures and fens, preferring slightly alkaline and moderate nutrient conditions. Coastally on dune slacks and the top end of salt marshes.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles it is mainly in Ireland, Wales and Scotland, on the wetter coasts. In Oxfordshire it is widely distributed.
#### Trends
It has decreased slightly in England especially in the east. In Oxfordshire has been found in four new sites and refound at Dry Sandford Pit SSSI BBOWT reserve since 2000, but not refound on Port Meadow SAC.
#### Conservaton work
Clearance of scrub by BBOWT at Dry Sandford Pit.
