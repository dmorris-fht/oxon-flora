### *Gentianella amarella* Autumn Gentian 
(L.) Börner
#### Ecology
This rosette-forming biennial grows in short calcareous grasslands with extrememly low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly a southern plant on the chalk, but also coastal in the north. In Oxforshire most abundant in the Chilterns, but also on the Wessex Downs and the Cotswolds, rare on the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at 61 sites, in Killick et al. (1998) at 63 tetrads; but in 2000-22 at c. 31 in Oxfordshire and c. 28 in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
