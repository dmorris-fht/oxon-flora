### *Alopecurus aequalis* Orange Foxtail 
Sobol.
#### Ecology
A low growing perennial grass of wet places; most often on muddy pond edges with very high nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread but much declined in central and eastern England and Wales. scattered over the county. Killick et al. (1998) had no confirmed records.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed three localities, Killick et al. (1998) had no confirmed records. In 2000-22 it is known at two patches on Otmoor.
#### Conservaton work
It has not been refound at the overgrown reservoir at Clattercote, and appeared in the restored arable on Otmoor.
