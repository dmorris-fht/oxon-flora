### *Lysimachia foemina* Blue Pimpernel 
(Mill.) U.Manns & Anderb.
#### Ecology
This species seems to be confined to arable land, unlike A. arvensis, which also occurs in semi-natural habitats.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in central and eastern England. In Oxfordshire moslty in the Cotswolds.
#### Trends
Killick et al. (1998) noted 17 tetrads for A. foemina and 8 for the blue  form of A. arvensis. During 2000-22 eight localiteis were recorded in the county and seven in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
None.
#### Variation
In Oxfordshire blue forms have been recorded since J Parkinson noted it in 1640, but it was not distinguished from the blue form of A. arvensis till the late 20th century.
