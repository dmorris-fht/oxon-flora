### *Cerastium diffusum* Sea Mouse-ear 
Pers.
#### Ecology
This tiny coastal plant appears to be a casual with us. It favours alkaline soils with low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly coastal throughout the Brisitsh Isles, but also rare inland. Only ever scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Not recorded by Druce (1927). Only ever recorded from eight sites in Oxfordshire. Known at two sites during 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
None.
