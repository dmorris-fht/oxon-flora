### *Descurainia sophia* Flixweed 
(L.) Webb ex Prantl
#### Ecology
This is an annual which grows to 70 cm. It is found on light soils in fields and waste places with neutral pH and raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
It is widespread in Britain, with concentrations in the east. Mostly in the Chilterns in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 12 sites, and Killick et al. (1998) eight tetrads mostly around Oxford. In 2000-22 recorded at five sites in Oxfordshire, all of which seem to be new, suggesting this species is a mobile casual.

None.
