### *Glyceria declinata* Small Sweet-grass 
Bréb.
#### Ecology
This perennial grows in marshy fields, waterside mud and shallow water. It prefers neutral soils with raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widely common throughout the British Isles. Widespread in Oxfordshire except the southwest.
#### Trends
Reported by Druce (1927) at two sites as a variety of G. plicata. HJK mistrusts many of the 46 tetrads reported in Killick et al. (1998). In 2000-22 at 11 sites in the county.
