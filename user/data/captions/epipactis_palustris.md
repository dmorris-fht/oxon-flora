### *Epipactis palustris* Marsh Helleborine 
(L.) Crantz
#### Ecology
This rhizomatous species is found in neutral to calcareous, but nutrient poor fens, especially those areas that are seasonally flooded.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in England, Wales and Ireland, but rare in Scotland. In Oxfordshire mostly on the Corallian Ridge.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) reported 10 sites, Killick et al. (1998) had seven tetrads. During 2000-22 at nine sites in the county and four in vc23.
#### Conservaton work
At all the SSSI sites scrub control is ongoing.
