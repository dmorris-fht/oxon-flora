### *Potamogeton nodosus* Loddon Pondweed 
Poir.
#### Ecology
This aquatic perennial prefers very high alkalinity and raised nutrients. It favours rivers with a gravelly floor and reproduces vegetatively, but does not fruit in Britain.
#### Status & distribution
Restricted to the Rivers Loddon, the Thames, Dorset Stour and Bristol Avon.
#### Trends
Historically in the River Loddon and the Thames between Wargrave and Bolney, in 2002 it reappeared in vc 22 at Wargrave and in 2017 in the Thames at Henley.
#### Conservaton work
None; cleaner water in the Thames may be favouring it.
