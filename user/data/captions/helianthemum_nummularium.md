### *Helianthemum nummularium* Common Rock-rose 
(L.) Mill.
#### Ecology
This small shrubby perennial grows in calcareous grassland with very low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly on the chalk and limestone in England and Wales, and widely in eastern Scotland. In Oxfordshire confined to the Wessex Downs, the Chilterns, the Corallian Ridge and the Cotswolds.
#### Trends
Druce reported c. 80 sites and Killick et al. (1998) 138 tetrads.
#### Conservaton work
None.
