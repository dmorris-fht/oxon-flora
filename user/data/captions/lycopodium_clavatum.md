### *Lycopodium clavatum* Stag's-horn Clubmoss 
L.
#### Ecology
This prostrate evergreen perennial favours extremely acidic and very low nutrient, but sometimes base-rich, soils.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread on acidic soils. In Oxfordshire mostly on the Chilterns plateau, long extinct on Shotover.
#### Trends
Now mostly at higher altitudes;  Druce listed four sites in the Chilterns, but last seen 1975. It appeared for a while on the Wardington railway, probably imported with ballast.
#### Conservaton work
None.
