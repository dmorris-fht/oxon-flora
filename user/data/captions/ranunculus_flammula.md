### *Ranunculus flammula* Lesser Spearwort 
L.
#### Ecology
This small perennial grows on mud and wet meadows, on neutral and low-nutrient soils.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the lowlands of the British Isles, with a stronghold in the Fens. Scattered in Oxfordshire, with a concentration east of the county, in the Ray and Thame catchments.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) described it as local and gave 42 localities, and Killick et al. (1998) gave 99 tetrads in vc 23. and during 2000-22 BSBI had c. 74 localities in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
None.
