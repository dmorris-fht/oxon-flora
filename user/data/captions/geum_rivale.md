### *Geum rivale* Water Avens 
L.
#### Ecology
This rhizomatous perennial is found in a wide variety of damp habitats: marshes, meadows and open woodland. It prefers neutral conditions with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Abundant in the north and west, but rare in the southeast and southern Midlands. Widespread in Oxfordshire, but mainly in the River Ray catchment.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 18 localities and Killick et al. (1998) 21 tetrads, During 2000-22 known at only at eight sites in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
#### Variation
var. pallidum ((Fis. & Mey) Blytt with pale yellow petals and green calyx and style tips in Woodeaton Wood (Brenan, 1946).
