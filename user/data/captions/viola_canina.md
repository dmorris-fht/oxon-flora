### *Viola canina* Heath Dog-violet 
L.
#### Ecology
Found in acid heathland and fens on extremely nutrient-poor soils.
#### Status & distribution
Throughout the British Isles, often coastal. Widespread in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at 28 sites, and Killick et al. (1998) reported 40 tetrads in vc 23 "on clay capping on chalk", though HJK doubted so many.  In 2000-22 reported at six localities in the county, but only confirmed at two.
#### Conservaton work
None.
