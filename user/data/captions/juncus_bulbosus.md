### *Juncus bulbosus* Bulbous Rush 
L.
#### Ecology
This small, tufted, perennial rush is found on wet heaths, pond margins, and heathy woodland rides. It favours peaty, acid, very infertile soils, often on bare soil or peat.
#### Status & distribution
Widely abundant throughout the British Isles except the east Midlands. In Oxfordshire mainly on the Chilterns plateau and the Corallian Ridge, also scattered in the north of the county.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 15 sites, and Killick et al. (1998) had 22 tetrads, and in 2000-22 there have been five localities in vc 23 and one in vc 22.
