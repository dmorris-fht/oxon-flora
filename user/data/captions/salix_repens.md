### *Salix repens* Creeping Willow 
L.
#### Ecology
This small shrub grows in a variety of damp, slightly acidic, low-nutrient habitats.
#### Status & distribution
Frequent in the north, west, and southern heaths of the British Isles. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed three sites in vc 23, Killick et al. (1998) had four tetrads; in 2000-22 only introduced plants known.
#### Conservaton work
Occasionally planted, as probably at Lodge Hill.
