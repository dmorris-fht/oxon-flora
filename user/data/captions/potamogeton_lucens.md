### *Potamogeton lucens* Shining Pondweed 
L.
#### Ecology
This robust, broad-leaved aquatic prefers calcareous water with moderate nutrient levels.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in central and eastern England and Ireland.
#### Trends
Druce said it was common in all districts. Killick et al. (1998) had 34 tetrads. Now mostly lost from the Thames it was found during the 1990s extensively along the Windrush, in the Evenlode (1 km above SP440148, 468398 and 487198) and the Cherwell (SP505148 by Killick).

None.
