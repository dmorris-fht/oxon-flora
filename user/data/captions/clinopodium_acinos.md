### *Clinopodium acinos* Basil Thyme 
(L.) Kuntze
#### Ecology
This is a small creeping annual, which favours extremely nutrient poor, well drained, highly calcareous sands or gravel where there is thin grass cover.
#### Status & distribution
Largely confined to the limestone and chalk of England. In Oxfordshire on the Cotswolds, Corallian Ridge, gravel terraces and Chiltern hills.
#### Trends
While Druce (1927) reported 65 sites, 75 tetrads were recorded in Killick et al. (1998), but only c. 28  in Oxfordshire in 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
None.
