### *Eleocharis acicularis* Needle Spike-rush 
(L.) Roem. & Schult.
#### Ecology
This very slender perennial grows in shallow water or in the drawdown zone and sets seed freely when above water. It prefers alkaline substrate and tolerates raised nutrient levels. Often in riverside sites flourishing on bare mud thrown up by dredging.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles. In Oxfordshire it was rare but widely distributed.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at seven sites and to Killick et al. (1998) near Eynsham and Binsey where Sibthorp found it, also at Burford, Blackbird Leys and Hethe.

None.
