### *Campanula rotundifolia* Harebell 
L.
#### Ecology
This species grows in dry grassland and heathland, with alkaline to acidic conditions and very low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, except in southwest England, central and eastern Ireland and the very north of Scotland. Throughout Oxfordshire, but mainly in the Chilterns and Cotswolds.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported it as locally common and widely distributed. Killick et al. (1998) listed 161 terads in vc 23. During 2000-22 recorded in c. 76 sites in Oxon and c. 65 in vc 23.
#### Conservaton work
Sown on the Chalk R Plots at Silwood Park and seen 1984 to 1990, then not till 2004 when it was occasional in R Plots 4 and 5 (Crawley 2005).
