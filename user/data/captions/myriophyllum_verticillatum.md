### *Myriophyllum verticillatum* Whorled Water-milfoil 
L.
#### Ecology
This submerged aquatic favours highly calcareous and nutrient-rich, still, or slow-moving, waters.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in England, Wales and Ireland, but commonest in the Fens. Scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Now absent from the Thames where it was common in Druce’s time (he had 26 localities). Killick et al. (1998) had 12 tetrads in vc 23; during 2000-22 in seven sites, all non-river.
#### Conservaton work
This species flourishes in the scrapes made on Otmoor by the RSPB.
