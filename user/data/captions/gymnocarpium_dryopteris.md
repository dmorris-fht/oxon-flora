### *Gymnocarpium dryopteris* Oak Fern 
(L.) Newman
#### Ecology
This is a small perennial fern of neutral soils with moderate nutrients; it frequents woodland edges.
#### Status & distribution
Now confined to the north and west in Great Britain.
#### Trends
Only ever at two sites (Shirburn and Pages bottom) in vc 23, last seen in 1887.
#### Conservaton work
None.
