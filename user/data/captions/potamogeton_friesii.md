### *Potamogeton friesii* Flat-stalked Pondweed 
Rupr.
#### Ecology
This aquatic perennial favours alkaline, still or slow-flowing water with raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly in central and eastern England, but with outposts in the Outer Hebrides, Orkney, and central Ireland. In Oxfordshire almost all in vc 22 Oxon.
#### Trends
In eight sites in vc 23 (Druce 1886). In Druce (1927) as P. mucronatus "Locally common in all districts except Ouse and Stour." Killick et al. (1998) had five tetrads, none since 1973. Crawley (2005) in a pool by Railway S Hinksey OXF 1975, R C Palmer. None since that record in 1975.

None.
