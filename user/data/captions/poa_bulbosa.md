### *Poa bulbosa* Bulbous Meadow-grass 
L.
#### Ecology
A small light-demanding perennial of neutral and very low nutrient soils.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly coastal and southern in the British Isles.  Rare in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Not known to Druce (1927) in vc 23 but in a wall at Cothill Mill in vc 22 (OXF). Originally in Stonesfield quarry, later in a quarry near Fawler, recently on a wall in Kidlington.
#### Conservaton work
None.
