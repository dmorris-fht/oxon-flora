### *Ulex minor* Dwarf Gorse 
Roth
#### Ecology
This small shrub needs extremely acidic and low nutrient soils.
#### Status & distribution
Strongly clustered in southeast England. In Oxfordshire it is now mostly restricted to the gravels of the Chilterns plateau, though also rare on the Corallian Ridge and in the northwest of the county.
#### Trends
In Oxfordshire formerly rare on the Corallian ridge and in the northwest of the county, but now it is restricted to the gravels of the Chilterns plateau.
#### Conservaton work
Two of the surviving sites are Local Wildlife Sites managed by volunteers.
