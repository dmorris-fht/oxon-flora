### *Platanthera chlorantha* Greater Butterfly-orchid 
(Custer) Rchb.
#### Ecology
This orchid is found in a wide range of habitats but usually on well drained calcareous soils with slightly raised nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered throughout the British Isles. In Oxfordshire mostly in the northern part of the county and on the Chilterns plateau.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) listed 35 sites, Killick et al. (1998) gave 43 tetrads. In 2000-22 at c. 20 localities in Oxfordshire.
#### Conservaton work
Detailed monitoring at the largest site was begun by Terry Swainbank and is being continued.
