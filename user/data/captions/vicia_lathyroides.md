### *Vicia lathyroides* Spring Vetch 
L.
#### Ecology
An early-flowering annual of dry infertile sandy or gravelly, neutral to acidic soils, mostly on coastal sand dunes.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly coastal, but also scattered in eastern England; absent from the west of Ireland and Scotland. In Oxfordshire on the Corllian Ridge, the Chilterns plateau and the Cotswolds.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported two sites and Killick et al. (1998) four tetrads in vc 23. During 2000-22 only at Sydlings Copse and in the Frilford area.
#### Conservaton work
None.
