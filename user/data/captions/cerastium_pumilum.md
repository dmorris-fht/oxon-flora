### *Cerastium pumilum* Dwarf Mouse-ear 
Curtis
#### Ecology
A small, easily overlooked winter annual of calcareous and extremely low nutrient substrates – short grass, banks and quarries.
#### Status & distribution
Scattered in England, Wales and southern Scotland.  In Oxfordshire mostly on the limestone to the northwest of the county.
#### Trends
Of a total of 16 recorded sites Druce reported six and only two are during 2000-22.
#### Conservaton work
None
