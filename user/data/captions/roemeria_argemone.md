### *Roemeria argemone* Prickly Poppy 
(L.) C.Morales, L.Mend. & Romero García
#### Ecology
This is an annual that is generally found on dry, well drained, calcareous, sandy or gravelly arable areas, now usually in field margins. It is susceptible to herbicides.
#### Status & distribution
A plant of central and southern England and the eastern seabord of the British Isles. Widespread in Oxforshire, but mainly on the Chilterns plateau and in the north of the county.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) gave it as "Not uncommon and widely distributed." Killick et al. (1998) cited 85 tetrads and BSBI gave 39 during 2000-22. This arable weed is not resistant to herbicides and survives increasingly in waysides and quarries.
