### *Valeriana dioica* Marsh Valerian 
L.
#### Ecology
This perennial grows in marshes with neutral pH and low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Absent from Ireland and northern Scotland but widespread in England and Wales. In Oxfordshire widespread, but mostly in the north of the county.
#### Trends
Known to Druce (1927) at 50 sites and at 76 tetrads to Killick et al. (1998). During 2000-22 at 31 sites in the county.
#### Conservaton work
None.
