### *Epilobium roseum* Pale Willowherb 
Schreb.
#### Ecology
A perennial willowherb of damp disturbed places, sometimes shaded, but it is often casual and sparse. It prefers slightly alkaline and high nutrient conditions.
#### Status & distribution
Widely distributed through the British Isles except in northwest Scotland and uplands. In Oxfordshire widely distributed.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) had only three sites as well as in Oxford, and Brenan (1946) deemed two colonies worthy of report, so Killick doubted many of the 59 tetrads (Killick et al. 1998 p.177).  R C Palmer saw it at two sites, H J M Bowen none and Killick not until 1997 OXF. During 2000-22 at five sites.
#### Conservaton work
Tree removal at Spartum Fen has been substantial, and grazing keeps the fen open at Sydlings Copse.
