### *Groenlandia densa* Opposite-leaved Pondweed 
(L.) Fourr.
#### Ecology
This aquatic prefers slowly moving alkaline water with moderate nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
In the British Isles mostly in the south and east of England. Widespread in Oxfordshire, though mainly in the northwest.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported 35 localities, Killick et al. (1998) 19 tetrads, and in 2000-22 eight localities in the county.

None.
