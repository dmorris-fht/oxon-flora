### *Viola palustris* Marsh Violet 
L.
#### Ecology
This is a small perennial which has long runners. It grows in wet, acid bogs and marshes with extremely low nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
A plant of the north, west and coastal areas of the British Isles. In Oxfordshire only on the Corallian ridge.
#### Trends
Druce (1886) listed two sites and wrote "A plant which another decade will probably remove from our list." and it did, no further records were made from vc 23. Bowen (1968) saw it at  Cothill and two sites on Boars Hill.

None.
