### *Lepidium heterophyllum* Smith's Pepperwort 
Benth.
#### Ecology
This small biennial or perennial prefers dry heaths, open anthropogenic sites, and tolerates some nutrients.
#### Status & distribution
Widespread in the British Isles, especially Scotland, Wales and the southwest of England, and the east coast of Ireland. Widely scattered in Oxfordshire.
#### Trends
Druce (1927) reported two sites and Killick et al. (1998) six tetrads. During 2000-22 only at two sites in Oxfordshire and one in vc 23.

None.
