### *Scutellaria minor* Lesser Skullcap 
Huds.
#### Ecology
A small perennial of acidic and very low nutrient wet heaths.
#### Status & distribution
Mostly confined to the south and west of the British Isles. No recent confirmed records on likely sites.
#### Trends
In Druce's time it was reported only at  Binfield Heath, Beckley and Cholsey, but lost by 1927. There are no recent confirmed records on likely sites.
#### Conservaton work
Attempts have been made to encourge the owners of Binfield Heath to allow clearance of some of the Rhododendron, but without success.
